<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>润娥出演古装剧 承诺到军营探望任时完</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707040454541487-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>《王在相爱》主演们出席新剧发布会。图为主演润娥（亦译：允儿）与任时完合影。（newsis提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年07月04日讯】（大纪元记者马维芬综合报导）由韩星<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%A6%E5%A8%A5.md#1">润娥</a>（陆译：<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%81%E5%84%BF.md#1">允儿</a>）、<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%BB%E6%97%B6%E5%AE%8C.md#1">任时完</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E5%AE%97%E7%8E%84.md#1">洪宗玄</a>联袂主演的古装剧《<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%9C%A8%E7%9B%B8%E7%88%B1.md#1">王在相爱</a>》于3日在首尔举行了制作发布会，剧中主要演员任时完、润娥、洪宗玄、吴闵硕等人皆出席了这场活动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9353026" style="width: 503px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707040315051487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9353026" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707040315051487-600x382.jpg" alt="" width="503" b="320" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%8E%8B%E5%9C%A8%E7%9B%B8%E7%88%B1.md#1">王在相爱</a>》主演们出席新剧发布会。图为主演们合影。（newsis提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9353028" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707040315171487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9353028" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707040315171487-600x331.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="276" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《王在相爱》主演们出席新剧发布会。图为主演们合影。（newsis提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>记者会上，<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B6%A6%E5%A8%A5.md#1">润娥</a>以一身粉色系洋装亮相，成为全场瞩目焦点。而当<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BB%BB%E6%97%B6%E5%AE%8C.md#1">任时完</a>聊及即将入伍的心情时，他则淡定地表示，不惧怕军队生活，因为润娥会去看望他，随即引来一片笑声。一旁的润娥也爽快地一口答应，还约定“收视红盘，我就穿戏服去看他！”</p>
<p>韩联社报导，这也是润娥首次接演的古装剧，她自认还有很多东西需要学习和感受，并称赞任时完是最棒的“演技派偶像”。为此，她还特别接受了武打动作的训练，力求尽量亲自上阵。</p>
<p>《王在相爱》是一部以高丽时代为背景的古装剧，讲述了三个男女间错综复杂的爱情与欲望的故事。该剧将于7月17日在韩国首播。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9353029" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707040315091487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9353029" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707040315091487.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="496" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《王在相爱》主演们出席新剧发布会。图为主演润娥（亦译：<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%81%E5%84%BF.md#1">允儿</a>）与任时完合影。（newsis提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9353117" style="width: 351px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707040315021487.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-9353117" title="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/07/1707040315021487.jpg" alt="" width="351" b="523" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">《王在相爱》主演们出席新剧发布会。图为主演<ahref="https://github.com/qqypmv3232/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%AA%E5%AE%97%E7%8E%84.md#1">洪宗玄</a>（左起）、润娥（亦译：允儿）与任时完合影。（newsis提供）</figcaption></figure>
