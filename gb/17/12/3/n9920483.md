<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：抬头看！超级月亮在全球各地登场</h2>
<a href="https://do0q2uqs2ncwe.cloudfront.net/X9eRwE6on"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pigrxl354/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/pigrxl354/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6>12月3日晚，全球各地的人们抬头看时，会看到一轮明亮的满月悬挂夜空，是今年最大满月。图为美国首都夜空的超级月亮。(NASA/Bill Ingalls via Getty Images)
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2017年12月04日讯】12月3日晚，全球各地的人们有机会看到一轮明亮的<ahref="https://github.com/pigrxl354/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BB%A1%E6%9C%88.md#1">满月</a>悬挂夜空。这是今年最大满月，也是80年来第8大满月，被称为“<ahref="https://github.com/pigrxl354/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E7%BA%A7%E6%9C%88%E4%BA%AE.md#1">超级月亮</a>”。</p>
<p>月球以椭圆形轨道绕行地球，所以和地球间的距离不断变化，如果<ahref="https://github.com/pigrxl354/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BB%A1%E6%9C%88.md#1">满月</a>发生时离地球越近，所看见的满月也越大。</p>
<p>美国科学家佩特罗表示，<ahref="https://github.com/pigrxl354/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E7%BA%A7%E6%9C%88%E4%BA%AE.md#1">超级月亮</a>（Super Moon）一般来说是指月球距离地球最近的状况，与平日的满月比较，超级月亮看来会大14%，亮度则高出30%。周日是黄历10月16，为“望月”、即“满月”之日。</p>
<p>周日这种情况下，月亮离地球约357,987公里，比今年6月份的满月离我们的距离近了差不多5万公里，</p>
<p>天文学家表示，周日晚的超级月亮会比今年6月9日的最小满月大出14%左右，同时，今晚满月的亮度也会比平常高出15%左右。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9921072" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9921072" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884958594.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9921072" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884958594-600x477.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="477" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9921072" class="wp-caption-text">12月3日晚，全球各地的人们抬头看时，会看到一轮明亮的满月悬挂夜空，是今年最大满月。图为美国首都夜空的超级月亮。(NASA/Bill Ingalls via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9920490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9920490" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884779744.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9920490" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884779744-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9920490" class="wp-caption-text">以色列内坦亚夜空的超级月亮。(JACK GUEZ/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这次的超级月亮于中港台时间12月3日晚上11点47分正式登场，时值美东时间12月3日上午10点47分，因为美国东部时间和中国的时差相差了13个小时。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9921126" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9921126" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884779762-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9921126" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884779762-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9921126" class="wp-caption-text">以色列内坦亚夜空的超级月亮。(JACK GUEZ/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9920489" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9920489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884755826.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9920489 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884755826-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9920489" class="wp-caption-text">香港上空的超级月亮。(ANTHONY WALLACE/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9920488" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9920488" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884755616.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9920488" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884755616-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9920488" class="wp-caption-text">香港上空的超级月亮。(ANTHONY WALLACE/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>欣赏超级月亮的最佳时间，是在当地的日落之后几分钟、月亮升起时，会使它看起来比高挂在天空时更大、更亮。</p>
<p>天文学家表示，民众赏月不需任何工具，用肉眼就可欣赏，但若用望远镜，则可看出一些月面地形特征。</p>
<figure id="attachment_9920487" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9920487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884755068.jpg"><img class="wp-image-9920487 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884755068-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9920487" class="wp-caption-text">缅甸夜空的超级月亮。(YE AUNG THU/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_9920486" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9920486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884755024.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9920486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2017/12/GettyImages-884755024-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-9920486" class="wp-caption-text">缅甸夜空的超级月亮。(YE AUNG THU/AFP/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>身在国际太空站的宇航员也在推特上贴出从太空看超级月亮的照片，宇航员Randy Bresnik写道，从太空站前排座位看去，美丽的月亮升起过程，令你感受到超级月亮的激励。</p>
