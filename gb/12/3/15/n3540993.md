<p><B>【新闻关键字】</B><br />1.	<B>matinee</B>: [?m?t?nˋe] n. 午后场、日场<br />2.	<B>sold out</B>:[ˋsoldˋa?t] v.phr. 卖光<br />3.	<B>Botanical </B>: [boˋt?n?kl] adj.植物的 <br />4.	<B>caliber</B>:[ˋk?l?b?] n. 水准, 程度<br />5.	<B>authenticity</B>:[??θ?nˋt?s?t?] n. 真实性、可信赖性<br />6.	<B>flavor </B>: [ˋflev?] n.韵味、风味 <br />7.	<B>delightful　</B>:[d?ˋla?tf?l] adj.令人心快的<br />8.	<B>fabulous </B>: [ˋf?bj?l?s] adj.惊人的、极好的 <br />9.	<B>rhythm</B>:[ˋr?e?m] n. 旋律、节奏</p>
<p>In St. Louis, Missouri, Shen Yun Performing Arts&#8217; Sunday afternoon <B>matinee</B> performance completely<B>sold out</B>.<br />在密苏里州圣路易斯市，神韵艺术团在下午的演出票，完全售罄。</p>
<p>Peter and Patricia Raven, from the Missouri <B>Botanical</B> Garden, were in the audience.<br />来自密苏里植物园的彼得与派翠，在观众群中。</p>
<p>[Dr. Patricia Raven, Trustee of Missouri Botanical Garden]:<br />“I’ve been to China 27 times, and I have never seen a show like this.”<br />派翠?瑞文博士说：“我到中国27次，我从未看过这样的一场表演。”</p>
<p>Dr. Raven commented on the<B> caliber</B> of the show.<br />瑞文博士评论这场演出的水准。</p>
<p>[Dr. Pat Raven, Trustee of Missouri Botanical Garden]:<br />“Enough of the cultural <B>authenticity</B> to show the cultural <B>flavor</B> of different ethnic groups, but with enough of the Broadway in it to make it spectacular.”<br />密苏里植物园总裁夫人帕特?瑞文博士：“这里包含着充份及纯正的文化，来展现不同民族的特色，同时又有如百老汇般的娱乐性，使这场演出非常壮丽。”</p>
<p>Her husband also enjoyed the performance.<br />她的先生也喜爱这场表演。</p>
<p>[Peter Raven, President of Missouri Botanical Garden]:<br />“Beautiful dancing and beautiful scenery. It brings to mind so many beautiful things about Chinese culture.”<br />帕特?瑞文博士“美丽的舞蹈、美丽的视觉，带给我们如此美丽的中国文化。”</p>
<p>[Marilyn Fredericks, Audience Member]:<br />“It is fantastic. The costuming is amazing, the music is <B>delightful</B>. It’s is so, so fabulous to see.”<br />观众玛丽莲?弗莱德瑞克：“绝对引人入胜！服装美丽竞艳、音乐动听，非常非常美妙绝伦。”</p>
<p>[Pat Young, Retired Nurse]:<br />“I loved it. It was <B>fabulous</B>, just fantastic. I cannot believe how much beauty and <B>rhythm</B> was in it. It was really good.”<br />退休护士帕特?杨：“我很喜爱，极好，就是太棒了，如此美丽，富有节奏，令人难以置信。真的太好了！”</p>
