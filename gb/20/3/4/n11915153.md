<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：清晨弄潮</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/09/31104d1050f75dad1dd4d20b3b32d1fa-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图为好美里好美的沙滩。 （蔡上海／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>为了得以就近亲自尝味清晨弄潮的乐趣，我们昨夜来到了海滨，住在海滨的旅社里。</p>
<p>躺在床上，海的歌音，始初是一曲催眠曲，从海边传来，在我耳边轻轻吟唱着，吟唱得连床铺都在我的矇眬意识中，成了一个摇篮，不停地轻摇缓荡。整夜，我把身子交给了这个摇篮，把耳朵交给了海的催眠曲，把眼睛交给了幽暝……。</p>
<p>当从遥远的矇眬中醒来，只觉空际已有曙光的消息在传播；海的催眠曲已唱罢，换上了一曲晨歌，节拍简短、轻快、活泼、悠扬，闻之，令身心一爽，精神振奋。</p>
<p>我们便在微微的曙光中奔向海边……。</p>
<p>一到海边，只见海面在微微的曙光中呈现着一片深色的宽大胸脯，正在一起一伏。那是海在做深呼吸吧！随着海一次又一次的深呼吸，海水便掀起一阵又一阵的海潮，在海边一起一伏，一涨一落；每一起涨，海潮便是一条白色绸缎，在微明中显得白而亮丽，然后碎没，退落，再翻掀成一阵新的海潮……。</p>
<p>海潮有起有伏，有涨有落，达到高潮又趋向低潮，然后又从低潮升向高潮；人生不也如此？</p>
<p>我们冲向海潮……。</p>
<p>海潮以它们白色的绸缎加在我们的双脚，我们的身上……。<br />
海潮以它们的白色浪花，喷满我们一身，缀满我们一身……。<br />
海潮以它们丰厚多情的双唇，亲吻遍吻我们的双脚……。<br />
海潮以它们柔软轻灵的手，伸向我们，献给我们以轻抚……。</p>
<p>从海潮和我的肌肤接触时起，一股清凉便从我的肌肤，直传入我的内心深处，遍及全身，而一股舒畅也是的，还有，一股满足，一股激昂和一股愉悦……。</p>
<p>还有什么闷热、苦恼、烦忧？已被全部溶融殆尽，抛诸九宵云外了。</p>
<p>我遂和大家欢呼了起来。欢呼声中也飞溅着所有的舒畅和满足，所有的愉悦和激昂。</p>
<p>我们冲向海潮，又从海潮里后退，又冲向海潮，又从海潮里后退……。</p>
<p>力在挥发着。欢乐洋溢在各处。笑声和叫声共海潮音齐飞……。</p>
<p>这是一片混乱的秩序。</p>
<p>摔倒了，就爬起来，继续弄潮，有的人甚至干脆顺势赖在海潮里，让它浸个够，享受海潮的亲抚，双唇的亲吻……。</p>
<p>这里是一片混乱的秩序。水沫一再喷上人的身体。喷上人的脸……。</p>
<p>曙光在我们一次一次冲向海潮又从海潮后退之间，渐渐来临；逐渐地，早晨的脸出现了，太阳在东方升起来了，光明已普遍来到人间，我们便结束了这一场清晨弄潮，将所有清晨弄潮的一切还给海边，将海和海滩还给太阳，还给即将到来的热浪……。</p>
<p><strong> Play with the Tide at Dawn</strong>&emsp;Hsu ChiCheng<br />
In order to appreciate the taste of fun in playing with tide personally at dawn, we come to the seaside and stay in the hotel of the seaside yesternight.</p>
<p>Lay on the bed, the song of the sea is a lullaby at first, transmitted from the sea, recites gentle by my ears, recited it even the bed becoming into a cradle in my dim sense and swing softly. Whole the night, I delivered my body to this cradle, delivered my ears to the lullaby of the sea, delivered my eyes to the gloom…</p>
<p>When awake from the hazy faraway, I just feel that the news of the dawn propagates in the air; the lullaby of the sea is come to an end, replaces it is a morning mood, short in beat, agile, active, melodious, on hearing it, I feel comfortable, bestir ourselves.</p>
<p>We rush to the seaside in the tiny dawn…</p>
<p>As reach the seaside, we just see the sea surface appears a piece of deep wide breast on the slight dawn, is rising and falling. May it is the sea does deep breathing! The sea water rises a spell after a spell of sea tide follows the rising and falling of the sea, rising and falling , ebbing and flowing; with each rises, the sea tide is a piece of white silks fabric, seems white and bright in the faint light, and then smashes, ebbing, then lifts into a spell of new sea tide…</p>
<p>There is rising and falling, ebbs and flows the sea tide, it will be low tide after it reaches high tide, and then from low tide rises to high tide; is life not so?</p>
<p>We rush to the sea tide…</p>
<p>The sea tide adds white silk fabric on our feet, our body…<br />
The sea tide sprays on our whole body with their white spray, adorns our whole body…<br />
The sea tide kisses our feet with their thick lips…<br />
The sea tide strength their soft kin hands, offers us slight caresses…</p>
<p>From the beginning the sea tide touches my skin, a band of cool transmits my deep inner mind, extends whole my body, and also a band of cozy, and besides, a band of satisfy, a band of passion and pleasure…</p>
<p>Is there still any sweltering, vexed, worry? They all smelt exhausted, threw away.</p>
<p>I therefore hail together with all of us. There splashes whole the cozy and satisfy in the hail, whole the pleasure and passion.<br />
We rush toward the sea tide, and retreat from the sea tide again, and once more rush toward the sea tide, and once more retreat from the sea tide…</p>
<p>Power is play the role of. Happy is permeating everywhere. Laugh and hail fly together with the sea tide…</p>
<p>This is a part of the order of confusion.</p>
<p>Arouse while tumble, play with the tide again succession, someone even hang on the tide on the way, to soak enough, enjoy the caress of the tide, as well as the kiss…</p>
<p>It’s an order of confusion. The foam sprays on the body, sprays in the face…</p>
<p>The dawn comes slowly from our rush toward the tide and retreat again; gradually, dawn appeared, sun rises from the east, brilliance is everywhere, so we make play with the tide comes to an end, back all play with the tide to the sea side, the sea and sea beach to sun, back to the coming heat wave…@</p>
