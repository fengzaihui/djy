<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>狗狗也需要穴道按摩 在家就能做的身体保健</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13218492-shutterstock_349899830-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>趁狗狗放轻松、自己心情也很悠闲时，试着帮狗狗做穴道按摩，保健身体。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>用小小的习惯，让宠物生活更健康。趁狗狗放轻松、自己心情也很悠闲时，试着一边抚摸狗狗、一边帮它做身体保健吧！</p>
<h2><strong>经络<ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%B4%E9%81%93%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9.md#1">穴道按摩</a></strong></h2>
<p>“经络”在中医观点里，是指让身体的气血循环。位于身体的14条经络上有经穴（穴道），藉由刺激穴道的方式就能调整身体、增进健康。在彼此都放松时，用温热的手开始进行，一边确认狗狗会不会讨厌。</p>
<p>把手指放在穴道上，数“1、2、3”加强力道，确认狗狗有没有感觉到痛。维持3秒不动，再数“1、2、3”放松力量。一个穴道要反复按压3～5次。</p>
<p><strong>①</strong><strong>委中穴</strong></p>
<p>对腰痛有效。位于膝盖的正后方。一边用大姆指以外的4根手指头支撑着膝盖的前侧，一边用大姆指往前方按压（左右脚各6次）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216638" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216638-P.114-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216638" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216638-P.114-1-450x308.jpg" alt="狗狗按摩" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216638" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的经络<ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%B4%E9%81%93%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9.md#1">穴道按摩</a>。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>②</strong><strong>腰的百会穴</strong></p>
<p>有防止老化、消除焦虑不安、整肠的作用。按压骨盆最宽处和背骨交会、手指可以最深入的部位（3～5次）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216640" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216640" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216640-P.114-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216640" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216640-P.114-2-450x308.jpg" alt="狗狗按摩" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216640" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的经络穴道按摩。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>③</strong><strong>肩井穴</strong></p>
<p>对解除肩膀僵硬很有效。举起前脚时可以在肩膀内侧摸到的穴道。用食指、中指、无名指这3根手指轻轻地按压（两侧各3～5次）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216644" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216644" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216644-P.114-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216644" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216644-P.114-3-450x308.jpg" alt="狗狗按摩" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216644" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的经络穴道按摩。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>④</strong><strong>肾俞穴</strong></p>
<p>可防止老化、腰痛、泌尿器官等问题。位于从最下面的肋骨数来第2节腰椎的两侧。用大姆指和食指从两侧按压（3～5次）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216646" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216646" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216646-P.114-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216646" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216646-P.114-4-450x308.jpg" alt="狗狗按摩" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216646" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的经络穴道按摩。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B7%8B%E5%B7%B4%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9.md#1">淋巴按摩</a></strong></h2>
<p>按摩淋巴可以帮助身体排出不需要的老废物质，对消除疲劳、肩膀酸痛或减轻压力等也有帮助。建议在狗狗放松时进行，按摩的同时要一边确认爱犬是否觉得舒服。只要有抚摸狗狗身体的动作都要轻柔，如果你的两手冰冷，请先摀热再触摸。</p>
<p>藉由<ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B7%8B%E5%B7%B4%E6%8C%89%E6%91%A9.md#1">淋巴按摩</a>促进全身淋巴的循环，1天1次、1次10分钟左右，就能为狗狗打造不易生病的体质。</p>
<p><strong>①</strong><strong>最终出口</strong></p>
<p>在淋巴最终出口的左肩胛骨前侧，用手指头从上往下搓揉按摩（6次）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216648" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216648-P.115-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216648" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216648-P.115-1-450x307.jpg" alt="狗狗按摩" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216648" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的经络穴道按摩。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>②</strong><strong>背部</strong></p>
<p>把手掌拱起，从脖子往尾巴的方向，碰碰碰轻拍背骨的两侧（6次）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216649" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216649" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216649-P.115-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216649" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216649-P.115-2-450x308.jpg" alt="狗狗按摩" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216649" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的经络穴道按摩。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>③</strong><strong>腋窝淋巴结</strong></p>
<p>轻轻揉一揉腋下的根部（6次）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216650" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216650-P.115-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216650" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216650-P.115-3-450x307.jpg" alt="狗狗按摩" width="450" b="307" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216650" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的经络穴道按摩。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>④</strong><strong>鼠蹊部淋巴结</strong></p>
<p>用手指按压大腿内侧，两只脚都要进行（6次）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216651" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216651-P.115-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216651" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216651-P.115-4-450x308.jpg" alt="狗狗按摩" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216651" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的经络穴道按摩。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>⑤</strong><strong>膝窝淋巴结</strong></p>
<p>用双手像抓住膝盖的上下一样交互搓揉。两只脚都要进行（6次）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216652" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216652-P.115-5.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216652" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216652-P.115-5-450x308.jpg" alt="狗狗按摩" width="450" b="308" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216652" class="wp-caption-text">狗狗的经络穴道按摩。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E6%9C%AC%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9%E7%90%83.md#1">草本植物球</a></strong></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/pxeqnh3506/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%89%E6%9C%AC%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9%E7%90%83.md#1">草本植物球</a>是源于泰国、印度的传统医疗。把好几种的草本植物包在布里做成球状，加热之后压贴在身上，身体会慢慢地变得温热，僵硬的肌肉会放松。一般对于自律神经、荷尔蒙调整、肌肉疲劳和身体冰冷等有效。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216656" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216656" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216656-P.116-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216656" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216656-P.116-1-450x362.jpg" alt="草本植物球" width="450" b="362" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216656" class="wp-caption-text">草本植物球。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>使用方法</strong></p>
<p>使用犬用的草本植物球。按照说明书上记载的方法，把温热的草本植物球压贴在身体上，并暂时不动、待热度传达到身体。等到渐渐热起来，一边维持压贴的状态，一边慢慢地搓揉按摩。</p>
<p>*草本植物球的咨询处：Floralsmile https://floralsmile-animalherbs.com</p>
<p><strong>①</strong><strong>肩胛骨的前面</strong></p>
<p>从脖子肌肉到前脚肌肉，由上往下轻轻搓揉。左肩是淋巴的最终出口，所以建议一开始就进行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216666" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216666" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216666-P.116-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216666" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216666-P.116-2-450x416.jpg" alt="草本植物球" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216666" class="wp-caption-text">草本植物球使用方法。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>②</strong><strong>从脖子到背部的按摩</strong></p>
<p>以背骨为正中央，左右两边有肌肉，所以以骨头为中心，分别在右侧和左侧单侧按摩。从头后方经过肩、腰一直到尾巴根部，直直地用草本植物球抚摸按摩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216668" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216668" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216668-P.116-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216668" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216668-P.116-3-450x417.jpg" alt="草本植物球" width="450" b="417" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216668" class="wp-caption-text">草本植物球使用方法。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>③</strong><strong>肚子的按摩</strong></p>
<p>在表面轻轻抚摸般地按摩。便秘时像是画圈一样，在肚子画圆按摩。拉肚子的时候就以逆时针方向按摩。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13216670" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13216670" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216670-P.116-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13216670" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13216670-P.116-4-450x416.jpg" alt="草本植物球" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13216670" class="wp-caption-text">草本植物球使用方法。（苹果屋提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>④</strong><strong>温热淋巴结</strong></p>
