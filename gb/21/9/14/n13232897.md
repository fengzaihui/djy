<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加东自驾游：纽芬兰晨雾 库克船长遗址观日落</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232948-20210914_canada_helen_chou_710-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>纽芬兰的晨雾。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/21/8/31/n13199774.md#1">加东自驾游：果冻豆房子和纽芬兰最古老的灯塔</a></p>
<p>不知不觉，两个“私奔”女人已经在加东玩了一星期了。今天将要从<ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8A%AC%E5%85%B0.md#1">纽芬兰</a>岛的最东端一路西行，准备第二天坐船回新斯科舍省。今天的行程有点辛苦，要开近7小时的车，全程426英里。运气好的话可以在岛西赶上<ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E8%90%BD.md#1">日落</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13232927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232927" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232927-20210914_canada_helen_chou_702.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13232927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232927-20210914_canada_helen_chou_702-600x1067.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="450" b="800" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232927" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E4%B8%9C%E8%87%AA%E9%A9%BE.md#1">加东自驾</a>游今天的行程。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还好一路顺利，<ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%A5%E8%90%BD.md#1">日落</a>前到达了今天的目的地，Corner Brook（科纳布鲁克）。坐落在哈伯河(Humber River)河口的科纳布鲁克是<ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BA%BD%E8%8A%AC%E5%85%B0.md#1">纽芬兰</a>岛西北部的一座综合服务性城市，人口密度仅次于圣约翰市。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%93%E5%85%8B%E8%88%B9%E9%95%BF.md#1">库克船长</a>(Captain Cook)于1767年探测了科纳布鲁克，所以当地人在山上建了一个库克船长历史遗址。酒店前台告诉我们，那里也是观日落的最佳地点。所以尽管开了一天的车有点累，一听有好地方可以看日落，我们就像打了鸡血一样跑到山顶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13232934" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232934" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232934-20210914_canada_helen_chou_703.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13232934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232934-20210914_canada_helen_chou_703-450x608.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="450" b="608" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232934" class="wp-caption-text">位于科纳布鲁克的<ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BA%93%E5%85%8B%E8%88%B9%E9%95%BF.md#1">库克船长</a>青铜像。这里也是关日落最佳地点。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>到了山顶一看，哇！果然再累也要上来！山下科纳布鲁克与哈伯河一览无遗。夕阳从库克船长身后斜照过来，在地上拉出了长长的身影。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13232935" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232935-20210914_canada_helen_chou_704.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13232935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232935-20210914_canada_helen_chou_704-600x424.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232935" class="wp-caption-text">俯瞰科纳布鲁克与哈伯河。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我们俩在山顶静静的等待着太阳落山，听着海鸟归巢，满身的疲惫消失无踪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13232939" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232939-20210914_canada_helen_chou_705.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13232939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232939-20210914_canada_helen_chou_705-600x400.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232939" class="wp-caption-text">日落哈伯河。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13232940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232940-20210914_canada_helen_chou_706.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13232940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232940-20210914_canada_helen_chou_706-600x400.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232940" class="wp-caption-text">日落哈伯河。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13232941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232941" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232941-20210914_canada_helen_chou_707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13232941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232941-20210914_canada_helen_chou_707-600x401.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232941" class="wp-caption-text">流光溢彩的哈伯河。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13232943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232943" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232943-20210914_canada_helen_chou_708.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13232943" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232943-20210914_canada_helen_chou_708-600x401.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232943" class="wp-caption-text">日落哈伯河。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后一抹阳光消失在山后，我们也心满意足的下山了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13232945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232945" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232945-20210914_canada_helen_chou_709.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13232945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232945-20210914_canada_helen_chou_709-600x338.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232945" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%A8%E9%9B%BE.md#1">晨雾</a>里的好山好水。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>终于要和纽芬兰的好山好水说再见了！大清早出发开往码头，沿路的美景让我们开不动车子了。一路上车子停了又停，直到不能再停，否则就赶不上轮船了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13232948" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232948" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232948-20210914_canada_helen_chou_710.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13232948" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232948-20210914_canada_helen_chou_710-600x400.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232948" class="wp-caption-text">纽芬兰<ahref="https://github.com/blffzl3237/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%A8%E9%9B%BE.md#1">晨雾</a>里的好山好水。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13232950" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232950" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232950-20210914_canada_helen_chou_711.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13232950" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232950-20210914_canada_helen_chou_711-450x674.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232950" class="wp-caption-text">纽芬兰路边吸饱了露水的野花。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>连路边吸饱了露水的野花都那么美。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13232951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232951-20210914_canada_helen_chou_712.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13232951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232951-20210914_canada_helen_chou_712-600x329.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="600" b="329" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232951" class="wp-caption-text">纽芬兰晨雾里的好山好水。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13232952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13232952" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232952-20210914_canada_helen_chou_713.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13232952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13232952-20210914_canada_helen_chou_713-600x236.jpg" alt="加东自驾" width="600" b="236" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13232952" class="wp-caption-text">纽芬兰晨雾里的好山好水。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
