<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>秋季穿搭灵感  浪漫印花、格纹经典不败</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274163-GettyImages-1205789826-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>Michael Kors时装秀，大格纹披巾霸气低调。（JP Yim/Getty Images for Michael Kors）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p class="p1">【大纪元2021年10月2日讯】（大纪元记者林溪综合报导）秋意渐凉，在这个转变的季节，色调是朴实温暖的大地色系，但那些夏天随处可见的<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E7%BA%B9.md#1">格纹</a>、<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A1%E7%BA%B9.md#1">条纹</a>与花卉图样，则延续到秋天，巧妙的搭配平实的色彩，不再亮眼突出，却有着低调的愉悦。</p>
<h2 class="p2"><span class="s1"><b><ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%BC%E7%BA%B9.md#1">格纹</a></b></span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">无需更多介绍，绵延无尽的格纹简单却很有风格，整件格纹样式或以配件呈现都很有质感。格纹细分成多种，葛伦格纹（</span><span class="s2">Glen check</span><span class="s1">）以细线组成小格，小格再交织成大格子，适合彬彬有礼、微带距离的秋天，典雅又绅士。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13274159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13274159" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274159-GettyImages-51331535.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13274159" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274159-GettyImages-51331535-450x748.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="748" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13274159" class="wp-caption-text">伦敦时装周，英国时装师Betty Jackson设计的格纹连身裙，工整的格纹与身形贴合，产生不一样的美。（Gareth Cattermole/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">有着错视感的千鸟格纹曾流行于</span><span class="s2">19</span><span class="s1">世纪的英国上层社会，如今也广泛运用于外套、套装中，若不敢尝试，可与素色服装搭配千鸟格纹配件，如帽子、包包，显得精致而有格调，多了分艺术家的气息。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13274178" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13274178" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274178-GettyImages-1303876036.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13274178" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274178-GettyImages-1303876036-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13274178" class="wp-caption-text">米兰时装周，Max Mara呈现千鸟格纹报童帽，搭配墨镜与西装打造<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>风格。（Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images for Max Mara）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">菱纹也是格纹的变化款，工整中多了斜向线条的趣味，也因此拥有超脱普通格纹的延伸感。菱纹最适合作为宽松下身的图样，随着步伐律动能产生流畅的视觉效果，搭配素色上身和简单的配件，就能轻松完成抢眼而低调的风格。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13274167" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13274167" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274167-GettyImages-56786643.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13274167" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274167-GettyImages-56786643-450x733.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="733" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13274167" class="wp-caption-text">Michael Kors时装秀，菱纹长裤随步伐摇曳，别有一番趣味。（Mark Mainz/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<h2 class="p2"><span class="s1"><b><ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9D%A1%E7%BA%B9.md#1">条纹</a></b></span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">条纹的宽度、直条或横条各有不同特色，细条纹较为优雅细致、宽条纹给人活泼的色块感，搭配简单的裙裤都十分好看。此外，条纹上衣很适合作为吊带类内搭，比起素Ｔ多了一分特色和风格，却不会抢走吊带外搭的风采。</span></p>
<p class="p1">今年秋天，可以尝试直线条衬衫，能单穿也能当外套，若卷起袖子看起来率性有型，散发豪气的风格；冬天可以选择宽横条纹编织的毛衣，成为阴暗天色中缤纷的点缀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13274179" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13274179" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274179-GettyImages-52700695.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13274179" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274179-GettyImages-52700695-450x857.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="857" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13274179" class="wp-caption-text">澳大利亚时装师Carla Zampatti 以紧身条纹西装外套呈现简洁干练的风格。（Patrick Riviere/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">另外，若你刚好想买一套西装，趁机尝试条纹款吧！直向线条有着拉高身形、瘦身的视觉效果，对于塑造专业干练形象大有助益，又不像普通西装那样平凡，适合在公司的商务休闲日穿着。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13274180" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13274180" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274180-GettyImages-845405182.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13274180" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274180-GettyImages-845405182-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13274180" class="wp-caption-text">纽约时装周，中国设计师Vivienne Hu以白底黑条纹打造修身的西装<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%97%B6%E5%B0%9A.md#1">时尚</a>。（Frazer Harrison/Getty Images for Vivienne Hu）</figcaption></figure>
<h2 class="p2"><span class="s1"><b>印花</b></span></h2>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">花朵印花给人春日的印象，但秋天其实也相当适合，可以选择深色为底、浅色的印花款式，为萧飒秋日带来浪漫却更加沈稳的气息。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">碎花洋装也能多层次<ahref="https://github.com/wjcmfz366/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>，外加针织背心是换季之时值得一试的搭配，更增添几分知性，天气更寒冷时，可以套上宽松毛衣或棉质Ｔ</span><span class="s2">-shirt<span class="s1">，</span></span><span class="s1">露出</span><span class="s1">裙?也十分好看。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13274182" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13274182" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274182-GettyImages-1327107109.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13274182" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274182-GettyImages-1327107109-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13274182" class="wp-caption-text">巴黎时装周，迪奥秋冬高订时装秀以微带金属光的印花裙搭配黑色外套，整体造型正式却不无聊。（Thierry Chesnot/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">另一种稍具挑战性的搭配是于洋装中内搭长裤，中和碎花洋装的女人味，带来不一样的休闲感，同时增加保暖度；款式选择经典牛仔裤较不容易出错，或是搭配素色宽裤能穿出打底衬裙的层次感。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13274183" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13274183" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274183-GettyImages-918214496.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13274183" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13274183-GettyImages-918214496-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13274183" class="wp-caption-text">Michael Kors时装秀的裹身裙以墨绿底佐以白色碎花，整体优雅浪漫又不失秋天色彩。（JP Yim/Getty Images for Michael Kors）</figcaption></figure>
