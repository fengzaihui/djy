<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>秋天穿搭色彩 大地色系添暖意典雅不花俏</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13222363-GettyImages-467397667-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>梅赛德斯?奔驰时装周。红蓝米三色格纹交织出秋天的模样，领巾更是换季之时的必备单品。（Monica Schipper/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年09月11日讯】（大纪元记者林溪综合报导）风中捎来一丝凉意，时序已进入<ahref="https://github.com/oqjwys331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%A4%A9.md#1">秋天</a>，你准备好要迎接这金色的季节了吗？衣柜里的衣服也得随之转变了，快来看看我们为秋天订定的色彩建议与单品推荐吧！</p>
<p>大地色系是<ahref="https://github.com/oqjwys331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A7%8B%E5%A4%A9.md#1">秋天</a>的调色盘，枫叶红、落叶褐、芥末黄、棕色、金黄稻穗等色彩虽属萧瑟却略带暖意，视觉上十分舒服，将此大地色系延伸到衣着上，是你可以考虑的秋季<ahref="https://github.com/oqjwys331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>色彩。</p>
<p>以深浅不同的单品相互搭配，营造<ahref="https://github.com/oqjwys331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A4%9A%E5%B1%82%E6%AC%A1%E6%90%AD%E6%B3%95.md#1">多层次搭法</a>，就算同色系也不单调，反而增添温暖柔和的质感，也凸显你的搭配功力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13223325" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13223325" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13223325-GettyImages-511142224.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13223325" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13223325-GettyImages-511142224-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13223325" class="wp-caption-text">美国品牌 Ralph Lauren。（P Yim/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>以深色作为底色，搭配一件稍亮的大地色系上衣单品，让视觉焦点集中于脸上容颜，小小的对比营造些许冲突感，凸显个人风格。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13223326" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13223326" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13223326-GettyImages-463223596.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13223326" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13223326-GettyImages-463223596-450x676.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13223326" class="wp-caption-text">纽约时装周，品牌 Argyleculture。（Fernanda Calfat/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秋天也是风衣的季节，虽然只是微凉时节，不用担心内搭过多导致风衣太紧，可以选择合身的款式，搭配棕色、咖啡色既修身又典雅，下散的衣?，在举手投足之间多了一份从容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13222362" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13222362" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13222362-GettyImages-85200769.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13222362" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13222362-GettyImages-85200769-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13222362" class="wp-caption-text">意大利品牌 Massimo Rebecchi。（Vittorio Zunino Celotto/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>秋天也适合皮革外套。对于夏天而言，皮革的质感有时稍嫌厚重，但皮制单品必需合身才好看，因此冬天较难搭配，在春秋换季时节却刚刚好，最能发挥皮衣的美感。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13222360" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13222360" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13222360-GettyImages-1229041694.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13222360" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13222360-GettyImages-1229041694-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13222360" class="wp-caption-text">土耳其时装品牌 Gokhanyavas。（Ekrem Serif Egeli/Getty Images for IHKIB）</figcaption></figure>
<p>谈到换季，一定得提到多层次<ahref="https://github.com/oqjwys331/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%A9%BF%E6%90%AD.md#1">穿搭</a>的最佳选择“背心”。以实用而言，背心能保暖上半身，手臂不包覆多了活动性，也不会那么热；在搭配上较能营造层次感。</p>
<p>背心可分为开襟或整件式两大种，开襟若附有扣子，选购时要注意扣子细节，若是廉价感的塑胶扣子，可能会大大降低整体格调。另整件式可以考虑花纹，例如格纹、条纹都是素雅好驾驭的样式，搭配在素色上衣外头不显花俏，平常喜欢素色极简穿搭的人可以试试。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13222365" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13222365" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13222365-GettyImages-1177509905.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13222365" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13222365-GettyImages-1177509905-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13222365" class="wp-caption-text">法国奢侈品牌 Celine。（Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>现在也许还不到拿出毛衣的季节，但充满洞洞的疏针织衫已经可以穿上了，既有保暖作用，又能通风不致太闷热。松松的针织衫穿在身上，利用材质的垂坠特性勾勒身体线条，不分身型都可穿着，下半身搭配留有余裕的锥型裤，展现法式慵懒而不造作的优雅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13222367" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13222367" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13222367-GettyImages-604570514.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13222367" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/09/id13222367-GettyImages-604570514-450x675.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13222367" class="wp-caption-text">德国设计师品牌 Marcel Ostertag。（Lars Niki/Getty Images for IMG Fashion）</figcaption></figure>
<p>领巾搭配适合温差大的换季时节，能围在脖子上，也能绑于包包上成为风格装饰，或者可以融入编发之中，用途广泛，作为上班服装配件或度假穿搭都很吸睛。</p>
<p>格纹是从不过时的经典款，大格豪爽亮丽、小格细腻精致，无论是衣物或是配件单品，格纹都能成就好搭不出错的特质。</p>
<p>以上这些穿搭建议，期望带给你的生活多些规律感，却不失趣味，让你永保童心与拥有个人品味。<strong>?@*</strong></p>
