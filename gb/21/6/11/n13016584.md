<h6>2021 Nissan Armada Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年06月12日讯】Nissan Armada是车系中最巨大的，属全尺寸SUV，与之前测试的Chevrolet Tahoe及GMC Yukon是同一级别，只是这是日系的代表而已，钟情日本车的人士也可选择全尺寸SUV了！</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;"><a title="YouTube video player" src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/JZ6hi"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ffdgji348/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/ffdgji348/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/0MRy3pDpWsY" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></p>
<p><center></center></p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">测试的2021年款Nissan Armada，是属于2017年推出的第二代Armada车型，只是今年推出中期改款。事实上，Nissan Armada是基本上跟豪华品牌Infiniti的QX80是一样的，除了车头及尾闸设计不同之外，从车侧来比较，两车几乎一样。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13016600" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13016600" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016600-2021_Nissan_Armada_08-e1623456362882.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13016600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016600-2021_Nissan_Armada_08-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13016600" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Nissan Armada Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">虽然中期改款后的Armada仍是采用一具5.6公升排气量的V8引擎，但其400匹马力比旧有的390匹，增加了一点。而扭矩也由394磅呎提升至413磅呎，汽缸的压缩比维持11.2：1，再加上直喷技术〈Direct Injection Gas〉，除了减少“爆震”之外，也提高了引擎的效率及经济性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13016603" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13016603" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016603-2021_Nissan_Armada_20-e1623456400399.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13016603" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016603-2021_Nissan_Armada_20-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13016603" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Nissan Armada Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">升级后Armada仍使用7速自动变速箱，可靠性高，但与现时普遍都使用8速以上变速箱的对手，显得落伍。附加的+/-手动变速功能，行驶在长长的下坡路时，也可手动限制以低档行车，减少危险。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13016605" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13016605" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016605-2021_Nissan_Armada_23-e1623456422873.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13016605" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016605-2021_Nissan_Armada_23-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13016605" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Nissan Armada Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">新车的最大拖力，不论是两驱或四驱版本，都是可达到8,500磅之高。虽然笔者测试期间并不需要拖拉东西，可是驾着这辆庞然大物，Armada的超强动力可令起步速度不迟缓，力量充足。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13016609" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13016609" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016609-2021_Nissan_Armada_33-e1623456471396.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13016609" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016609-2021_Nissan_Armada_33-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13016609" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Nissan Armada Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Armada车身高，座椅也处于高位，所以前座A柱内侧特设有扶手，以方便驾驶者及乘客扶着“攀登”上车，而且Armada也装有“踏脚板”，让上下车时更安全更方便。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13016606" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13016606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016606-2021_Nissan_Armada_26-e1623456433140.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13016606" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016606-2021_Nissan_Armada_26-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13016606" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Nissan Armada Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">进入车厢，空间宽敞，中控台看起来也特别巨大，前座两座位中间的变速杆及杯座，可以并排而又不会互相干扰。由于中控台宽阔，各按钮之间的空间也较多。顶部的12.3吋显示屏，比旧有8吋萤幕增大不少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13016604" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13016604" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016604-2021_Nissan_Armada_21-e1623456412897.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13016604" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016604-2021_Nissan_Armada_21-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13016604" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Nissan Armada Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">在天气冷时开车，拥有遥控打火功能可在上车前预热汽车，但记着不可在密封的车房内进行。在汽车启动之后，上车时需要马上开启“加热方向盘”及“加热座椅”，加热的速度也快，不消一会已是非常温暖了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13016607" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13016607" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016607-2021_Nissan_Armada_28-e1623456446399.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13016607" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016607-2021_Nissan_Armada_28-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13016607" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Nissan Armada Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">新车更有不少安全设备，例如前方碰撞警示〈Predictive Forward Collision Warning〉、侧镜盲点提示〈Blind Spot Intervention〉、倒车碰撞提示〈Backup Collision Intervention〉及鸟瞰式停车摄像等，而且汽车前后防撞都装上了雷达感应器，进出狭窄位置也有充足提示，不容易犯错碰撞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13016608" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13016608" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016608-2021_Nissan_Armada_30-e1623456459829.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13016608" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13016608-2021_Nissan_Armada_30-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13016608" class="wp-caption-text">2021 Nissan Armada Platinum。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">与前数周测试的Chevrolet Tahoe又或GMC Yukon，设备马力上虽然更胜一筹，毕竟Nissan Armada已是数年前的设计，与刚更新的Tahoe及Yukon相比，差一点是可以理解的。所以现时的Nissan Armada是钟情日系车的消费者，又或只对日系车品质有信心的人，否则，相信要等待Nissan推出下一代的Nissan Armada了。◇</p>
