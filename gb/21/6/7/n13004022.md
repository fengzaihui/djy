<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Safari Firearms拥澳洲最全枪械弹药 狩猎装备</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13004089-SAVAGE-A22MR-22WMR.jpg" />
<h6>Safari Firearms枪支店是澳洲枪械零售行业佼佼者，专卖枪支弹药和一系列狩猎装备。（Safari Firearms提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年06月07日讯】<ahref="http://safarifirearms.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Safari Firearms</a>枪支零售店为<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>本地的家族生意，由一间小商铺起家，现已发展为全国行业领先。无论是射击、狩猎还是户外运动，Safari枪店满足您的各类装备需求。</p>
<p>凭借可靠产品质量与细致客户服务，<ahref="http://safarifirearms.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Safari Firearms</a>成为<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%AA%E6%A2%B0.md#1">枪械</a>零售行业佼佼者，在当地枪支和狩猎爱好者中拥有甚佳口碑。</p>
<h2>Safari Firearms出售产品</h2>
<p>* <ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%B9%E8%8D%AF.md#1">弹药</a><br />
* 射击服装、迷彩服<br />
* 枪支及其配件<br />
* 狩猎用具<br />
* 各类瞄准镜<br />
* <ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BC%B9%E8%8D%AF.md#1">弹药</a>填充设备<br />
* <ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%AA%E6%A2%B0.md#1">枪械</a>辅助用具</p>
<figure id="attachment_13004087" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13004087" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13004087-MH12-10SHOT.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-13004087" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13004087-MH12-10SHOT.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="230" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13004087" class="wp-caption-text">Safari Firearms枪支店是<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2.md#1">澳洲</a>枪械零售行业佼佼者，专卖枪支弹药和一系列狩猎装备。（Safari Firearms提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>关于<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">新州</a><ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%81%E6%9E%AA%E6%89%A7%E7%85%A7.md#1">持枪执照</a></h2>
<p>在<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%B0%E5%B7%9E.md#1">新州</a>，下列5类枪支（A类与B类）的执照最易获取：</p>
<p>漆弹枪；<br />
气枪；<br />
凸缘式底火步枪（Rimfire Rifles）；<br />
中心底火步枪（Centrefire Rifles）；<br />
霰弹枪。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13004086" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13004086" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13004086-LEUPOLD-LTO-TRACKER-6X.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13004086" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13004086-LEUPOLD-LTO-TRACKER-6X.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="290" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13004086" class="wp-caption-text">Safari Firearms枪支店是澳洲枪械零售行业佼佼者，专卖枪支弹药和一系列狩猎装备。（Safari Firearms提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>申请新州<ahref="https://github.com/fucxso305/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8C%81%E6%9E%AA%E6%89%A7%E7%85%A7.md#1">持枪执照</a>必要步骤</h2>
<p><strong>第一步、 加入受认证的射击俱乐部</strong></p>
<p>根据新州警署枪支登记处（NSWFAR）规定，本州内所有持枪者都需为某个受认证射击俱乐部的会员。</p>
<p><strong>第二步、报名参加枪支安全课程</strong></p>
<p>Safari Firearms可在店内随时为有需要的顾客提供该课程。课程时间在周一至周五的早上10点到下午5点之间。</p>
<p><strong>第三部、登陆/创建一个新州政府服务平台（Service NSW）账号： <ahref="https://www.service.nsw.gov.au/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://www.service.nsw.gov.au/</a></strong></p>
<p>A、登陆后，在主界面“浏览服务”栏中点击进入“法律和警察服务”；<br />
B、在“法律和警察服务”一栏点击进入“枪支许可证”；<br />
C、点击“申请枪支许可证（个人）”；<br />
D、完成在线表格，上传（俱乐部）会员证明和枪支安全课程完成证书。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13004088" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13004088" style="width: 350px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13004088-PRO-HUNT-LITE-JACKET.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13004088" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/06/id13004088-PRO-HUNT-LITE-JACKET.jpg" alt="" width="350" b="598" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13004088" class="wp-caption-text">Safari Firearms枪支店是澳洲枪械零售行业佼佼者，专卖枪支弹药和一系列狩猎装备。（Safari Firearms提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>关于新州申请枪证的进一步信息请前往： <ahref="http://www.safarifirearms.com.au/firearms-license-in-nsw" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">www.safarifirearms.com.au/firearms-license-in-nsw</a></p>
