<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：世界冠军雕刻师将蔬果变成精美艺术品</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193958-Artist-1200x720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>意大利年轻艺术家丹尼尔?巴雷西擅长蔬果雕刻，在他精湛的雕工下，将原本平凡的蔬果转变成栩栩如生的动物、鸟类以及花朵等各式雕刻作品。（巴雷西提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年08月28日讯】（大纪元记者张雨霏编译报导）意大利一位才华横溢的年轻<ahref="https://github.com/swmvxl310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%AE%B6.md#1">艺术家</a>擅长<ahref="https://github.com/swmvxl310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%94%AC%E6%9E%9C.md#1">蔬果</a><ahref="https://github.com/swmvxl310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%95%E5%88%BB.md#1">雕刻</a>，在他精湛的雕工下，将原本平凡的蔬果转变成栩栩如生的动物、鸟类以及花朵等各式雕刻作品，他的精美作品吸引了世界各地许多美食家和艺术爱好者的关注。</p>
<p>年仅30岁的丹尼尔?巴雷西（Daniele Barresi）来自意大利的一个南部城镇，他是一名专业的糕点厨师，现在在悉尼工作。他从7岁开始便接触<ahref="https://github.com/swmvxl310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9B%95%E5%88%BB.md#1">雕刻</a>工艺，随着不断的练习和实践，他的雕刻功力也在不断地成长和完善。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13193966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193966" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193966-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-4.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193966-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-4.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="443" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193966" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13193967" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193967" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193967-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-9.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193967" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193967-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-9.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193967" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他在Bored Panda网站自述道：“每当我握着雕刻刀时，我的思绪会直达内心深处，然后再传递到手上”。正因为如此，巴雷西的作品都具有强大的生命力，将不可能化为可能，他自己将整个雕刻过程形容为“像变魔术一样”。</p>
<p>近期，这位<ahref="https://github.com/swmvxl310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF%E5%AE%B6.md#1">艺术家</a>比较热衷利用千禧一代非常喜爱的<ahref="https://github.com/swmvxl310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E6%B2%B9%E6%9E%9C.md#1">牛油果</a>（也叫鳄梨）作为素材，创作出一系列令人难以置信的复杂雕刻作品，并且受到媒体的广泛关注。</p>
<p>如今，巴雷西可以在短短几个小时内，在<ahref="https://github.com/swmvxl310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%9B%E6%B2%B9%E6%9E%9C.md#1">牛油果</a>上雕刻出令人惊叹的动物和花朵等图案，细节之复杂令人惊叹，有时他还会充分利用牛油果巨大的果仁和深绿色的果皮作装饰，运用得恰到好处。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13193961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193961" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193961-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-5-1200x1200.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193961-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-5-1200x1200.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193961" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13193962" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193962" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193962-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-6.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193962" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193962-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-6.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="565" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193962" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13193964" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193964" style="width: 453px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193964-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-7.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193964" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193964-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-7.jpg" alt="" width="453" b="566" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193964" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>此外，他也会用其它水果、蔬菜、奶酪甚至是肥皂进行创作。“我非常喜欢雕刻，尤其用西瓜进行创作，因为它们让我想起了自己的出生地”，他说道，“还因为它们的颜色——绿色、红色和白色，还有它们的大小，这样我可以选择多种设计方案。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_13193959" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193959" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193959-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-1.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193959" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193959-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-1.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193959" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13193960" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193960" style="width: 451px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193960-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-10.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193960" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193960-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-10.jpg" alt="" width="451" b="451" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193960" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>他的作品不仅在视觉上令人惊叹，而且充分展现了这位艺术家的专业技能。每件作品可能都需要数小时才能完成，但巴雷西享受创作过程中的每一刻：“就像人们常说的那样，如果你热爱自己正在做的事情，那么花多长时间都没关系，因为你会享受整个过程。”</p>
<p>凭借其创作的独特作品，巴雷西还赢得了许多奖项，他在2013年和2014年连续两届在WACS冠军赛（WACS Championship）中被评为<ahref="https://github.com/swmvxl310/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E5%86%A0%E5%86%9B.md#1">世界冠军</a>雕刻设计师。2015年，他又被邀请回到这项赛事担任了评委。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13193968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193968" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193968-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-11.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193968-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-11.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193968" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13193969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193969" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193969-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-12-1200x1167.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193969-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-12-1200x1167.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193969" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13193970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13193970" style="width: 452px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193970-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-13-1200x1500.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-13193970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/08/id13193970-ET-Daniele-Barresi-artwork-13-1200x1500.jpg" alt="" width="452" b="565" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13193970" class="wp-caption-text">（巴雷西提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>水果雕刻让巴雷西把他对食物的热爱和对艺术的热情结合起来。“雕刻能给我带来一种心灵的宁静”，他分享道，“我一直喜欢任何与艺术和厨房有关的东西，所以雕刻给了我将二者结合起来的可能性。”</p>
<p>巴雷西也会将自己的作品分享到<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/danielebarresi_artist/">IG</a>上，他目前已经吸引了超过88,000名粉丝。</p>
