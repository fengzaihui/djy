<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进非洲（4）塞伦盖蒂的动物乐园</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911176-20210428_africa_helen_chou_401-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>斑马的乐园 - 塞伦盖蒂国家公园一角（Serengeti National Park）。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/efqihy3582/djy/blob/master/gb/21/4/19/n12889592.md#1">走进非洲（3）非洲农家乐与塞伦盖蒂野生动物</a></p>
<p>人品爆表的我们心满意足的离开了正在吃大餐的狮子们，往公园深处开去。今晚我们就住在公园内。有没有可能与狮子们隔墙而眠？我还真不知道哎！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911175" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911175" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911175-20210428_africa_helen_chou_402.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12911175" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911175-20210428_africa_helen_chou_402-450x587.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="450" b="587" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911175" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/efqihy3582/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a>民乐。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一进渡假村就听到一阵节奏欢快的<ahref="https://github.com/efqihy3582/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a>打击乐，让人情不自禁的跟着鼓点舞动。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911174" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911174" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911174-20210428_africa_helen_chou_403.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911174" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911174-20210428_africa_helen_chou_403-450x320.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="600" b="427" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911174" class="wp-caption-text">渡假村内的小楼。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我一眼就爱上了这些茅草顶的房子。小楼周边风景漂亮，颇有身处野外之感。但房间里面却又整洁温馨，风格地道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911173" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911173" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911173-20210428_africa_helen_chou_404.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12911173" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911173-20210428_africa_helen_chou_404-450x600.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911173" class="wp-caption-text">渡假村的客房。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>入住时，前台领班认真关照我们：因为我们是在公园深处，天黑后不要独自行动。几个路口都有工作人员站岗，他们可以陪同我们去餐厅等等。或者先从房间打电话到前台，等工作人员前来护送。</p>
<p>说实话，我有点小紧张，也有点小期待。毕竟不是天天有机会路遇狮子大象什么的。至于我是不是叶公，那也要龙来了才知道是不是？</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911172" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911172" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911172-20210428_africa_helen_chou_405.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911172" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911172-20210428_africa_helen_chou_405-450x293.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="601" b="391" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911172" class="wp-caption-text">塞伦盖蒂的落日。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>太阳要落山了，我也不敢跑远，就在小楼前等待。寂静中仔细听了听，好像没有狮子吼。其实，在非洲最可怕的不是庞大的狮子大象，而是极毒的绿色曼巴蛇(green mamba)和小小的蚊子。在非洲可以说是谈“蚊”色变，因为它会传播疟疾。我们到非洲前打了不少防疫针，如黄热、伤寒、乙肝什么的，而在路上每天要吃的就是防疟疾的药。该药副作用极大，但不吃又不行。我们一行四人每个人的药性反应都不一样。每天早餐时分享各自的反应成了我们旅途中的一个有趣的话题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911171" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911171" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911171-20210428_africa_helen_chou_406.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911171" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911171-20210428_africa_helen_chou_406-450x337.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界，非洲" width="601" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911171" class="wp-caption-text">织布鸟(Weaver bird)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>早上出门才发现小楼旁有个织布鸟的杰作。其实它们不织布，而是雄鸟为了取悦雌鸟而编织的精致鸟巢：美女快看，哥哥我有漂亮的豪宅哦！嫁给我吧！嫁给我吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911169" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911169" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911169-20210428_africa_helen_chou_407.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12911169" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911169-20210428_africa_helen_chou_407-450x706.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="450" b="706" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911169" class="wp-caption-text">路边卖萌的长颈鹿。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这头长颈鹿正在树下吃早饭，看到我们的车就慢慢的踱过来，忽闪着长睫毛大眼睛，滴着口水卖萌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911168" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911168-20210428_africa_helen_chou_408.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911168" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911168-20210428_africa_helen_chou_408-450x300.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911168" class="wp-caption-text">南非大羚羊(Topi antelope)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>躲在灌木丛中吃早饭的南非大羚羊一看到我们，就迈着四方步像走T台似的走到空地当中，一个优雅转头亮相：我美吧！我脸长不？我大长腿上的袖套别致吧！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911166" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911166" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911166-20210428_africa_helen_chou_409.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911166" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911166-20210428_africa_helen_chou_409-450x300.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="602" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911166" class="wp-caption-text">斑鬣狗(Hyena)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下一个登场的就有些凶残了。斑鬣狗在西方文化与非洲传统宗教中都具有不好的名声。西方文化普遍认为斑鬣狗是丑陋且懦弱的动物。而在非洲传统宗教中，斑鬣狗被认为是贪婪、暴食、愚笨但又具有力量与潜在危险性的动物。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911165" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911165" style="width: 602px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911165-20210428_africa_helen_chou_410.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911165" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911165-20210428_africa_helen_chou_410-450x299.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="602" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911165" class="wp-caption-text">小斑鬣狗。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>你看，连这么小的幼仔都两眼冒凶光。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911164" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911164" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911164-20210428_africa_helen_chou_411.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12911164" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911164-20210428_africa_helen_chou_411-450x562.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="450" b="562" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911164" class="wp-caption-text">獴(Mangoose)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个也凶狠，名字叫獴。你别看它个子小小的，专吃蛇。还毒不死，最多昏过去睡一觉，30分钟后满血复活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911163" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911163" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911163-20210428_africa_helen_chou_412.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911163" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911163-20210428_africa_helen_chou_412-450x197.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="601" b="263" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911163" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/efqihy3582/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%91%E9%A9%AC.md#1">斑马</a>的乐园。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在非洲一年只有两季：雨季与旱季。三月初的塞伦盖蒂是<ahref="https://github.com/efqihy3582/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%91%E9%A9%AC.md#1">斑马</a>与角马的乐园。这些食草动物像割草机一样，一片片绿地在它们的嘴下消失。每年到了6、7月，坦桑尼亚大草原的青草被它们割完，一场地球上最壮观的动物大迁徙就会上演。数百万的动物大军长途跋涉三千多公里，碾过茫茫大草原，从塞伦盖蒂公园南部迁徙至肯尼亚境内的马赛马拉野生动物保护区。</p>
<p>途中，它们不仅要躲过狮子与豹的埋伏，还要拼命逃过张着大嘴聚集在马拉河两岸的鳄鱼。好不容易到了终点，短短2、3个月又要为了食物返回塞伦盖蒂。这一趟来回，只有半数不到的幸运者能回到出发地。而跟着它们一起回来的，是近50万头在惊险旅途中诞生的新生命。</p>
<p>这也是动物界的一个轮回。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911162" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911162" style="width: 596px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911162-20210428_africa_helen_chou_413.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911162" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911162-20210428_africa_helen_chou_413-450x228.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="596" b="302" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911162" class="wp-caption-text">斑马的乐园。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12911161" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911161" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911161-20210428_africa_helen_chou_414.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911161" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911161-20210428_africa_helen_chou_414-450x241.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="601" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911161" class="wp-caption-text">斑马与角马和平共处。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>看着眼前悠闲和谐的一幕，很难想像旱季为了各自生存的惨烈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12911160" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12911160" style="width: 601px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911160-20210428_africa_helen_chou_415.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12911160" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12911160-20210428_africa_helen_chou_415-450x227.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 非洲" width="601" b="303" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12911160" class="wp-caption-text">野餐归来的狮子们。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天最大的惊喜是在最佳时间赶到最佳地点，目睹一大家子的狮子野餐归来。一个老爷带着6、7个大小老婆和一众孩儿们从我们车边擦身而过。</p>
