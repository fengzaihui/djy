<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】五花肉炒苦瓜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869964-52922237faab7374a0173d3749e95397-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道苦瓜炒五花肉，不仅很下饭，而且具有清火之功效，非常适合春夏季食用。（普照荟苑提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>民间有一种说法：“苦瓜虽苦，但不传苦于他物。”意思是用苦瓜做菜，不管与什么菜肴混在一起，都不会把苦味传给别的菜。因此，苦瓜被称为“君子菜”，意思是它具备“君子之德、君子之功”。</p>
<p>苦瓜苦中带甜，风味独特，营养价值很高，有“天然胰岛素”的美称。苦瓜不仅维生素C含量丰富，还含有苦瓜甙、氨基酸、胡萝卜素、粗纤维以及磷、铁和多种矿物质。</p>
<p>中医认为，苦瓜味甘苦性寒，具有益气壮阳、滋肝养血、清热解毒、清心明目、止痢、润肺、补脾胃的功效。在日常生活中适当地食用，对高血压、心脏病、糖尿病、便秘、肾炎、前列腺增生、眼底出血、皮肤粗糙、咽喉肿痛、肥胖症、疥疮等大有裨益。</p>
<p>现代医学研究证明，苦瓜还具有降血糖、降血压、调节血脂、提高免疫力的作用。常吃苦瓜还能增强皮层活力，使皮肤变得细嫩健美。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道五花肉炒苦瓜，看似简单，做起来却并不简单，每一道工序都不能忽略，不仅五花肉和苦瓜都要事先腌制好，而且要大火翻炒，五花肉要翻炒两次，才能去掉多余的油脂，这样添加苦瓜再翻炒后，苦瓜恰到好处地吸收了猪油的香味，清香爽甜的独特风味发挥得淋漓尽致，加入配料爆香后，更是锦上添花，味道更加香浓。</p>
<p>罗厨认为，这道菜充满纯正的家乡口味，不仅很下饭，而且具有清火之功效，非常适合春夏季食用。</p>
<h2>五花肉炒苦瓜</h2>
<p>主料：五花肉、苦瓜</p>
<p>配料：红葱头、葱段、姜片、蒜头、蒜苗、红椒</p>
<p>调料：盐、糖、胡椒粉、白酒、生抽、老抽、生粉、油</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 将五花肉去皮切成肉片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869970" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869970-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12869970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869970-1-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869970" class="wp-caption-text">将五花肉去皮切成肉片。（<ahref="https://github.com/harlek3030/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 用盐、糖、胡椒粉、白酒、生抽等调料腌制肉片15分钟待用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869971" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869971-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12869971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869971-2-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869971" class="wp-caption-text">用调料腌制肉片。（<ahref="https://github.com/harlek3030/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%99%AE%E7%85%A7%E8%8D%9F%E8%8B%91.md#1">普照荟苑</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 将苦瓜洗净沥干水分，去心（核）切厚片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869973" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869973-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12869973" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869973-3-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869973" class="wp-caption-text">将苦瓜洗净切厚片。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 用海盐腌制10分钟后用开水烫苦瓜待用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869974" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869974-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12869974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869974-4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869974" class="wp-caption-text">用海盐腌制后用开水烫苦瓜。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 猛火阴油，将五花肉猛火焗香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869975" style="width: 437px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869975-5.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12869975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869975-5.jpg" alt="" width="437" b="288" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869975" class="wp-caption-text">将五花肉猛火焗香。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将五花肉捞出沥干油分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869976" style="width: 417px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869976-6.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12869976" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869976-6-450x290.jpg" alt="" width="417" b="269" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869976" class="wp-caption-text">将五花肉捞出沥干油分。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 利用镬底余油放入海盐、糖炒香后放入凉苦瓜爆香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869978" style="width: 413px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869978-7.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12869978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869978-7-450x302.jpg" alt="" width="413" b="277" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869978" class="wp-caption-text">海盐、糖炒香后放入凉苦瓜爆香。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 猛火阴油将蒜头、姜片爆香，放入五花肉再爆香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869980" style="width: 425px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869980-8.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12869980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869980-8-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="425" b="283" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869980" class="wp-caption-text">放入五花肉再爆香。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 放苦瓜一起炒香，用老抽调色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869982" style="width: 432px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869982-9.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12869982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869982-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="432" b="243" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869982" class="wp-caption-text">放苦瓜一起炒并用老抽调色。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 加入葱头、葱段、姜片等配料，猛火爆香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869984" style="width: 423px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869984-10.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12869984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869984-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="423" b="238" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869984" class="wp-caption-text">加入配料，猛火爆香。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 最后添加生粉水和包尾油即可盛盘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12869987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12869987" style="width: 411px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869987-11.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-12869987" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12869987-11-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="411" b="231" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12869987" class="wp-caption-text">添加生粉水和包尾油。（普照荟苑提供）</figcaption></figure>
