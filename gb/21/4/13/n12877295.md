<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>NBA奇才板凳出击 断爵士主场24连胜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877297-492770-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>波丹诺维奇。（Chris Graythen/Getty Images）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年04月13日讯】（大纪元记者魏文彬综合报导）虽然犹他<ahref="https://github.com/haatdn3351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B5%E5%A3%AB.md#1">爵士</a>双星米契尔（Donovan Mitchell）和波丹诺维奇（Bojan Bogdanovic）合力缴出75分，远超华盛顿<ahref="https://github.com/haatdn3351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%87%E6%89%8D.md#1">奇才</a>双卫比尔（Bradley Beal）及威斯特布鲁克（Russell Westbrook）的59分，但奇才板凳相挺，贡献59分，助队以125：121击败西区龙头，终止<ahref="https://github.com/haatdn3351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B5%E5%A3%AB.md#1">爵士</a>主场24连胜。</p>
<figure id="12877300" aria-describedby="caption-12877300" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877300-492775-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877300-492775-450x675.jpg" alt="威斯布鲁克，图为资料照。" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12877300" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/haatdn3351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A8%81%E6%96%AF%E5%B8%83%E9%B2%81%E5%85%8B.md#1">威斯布鲁克</a>，图为资料照。（Patrick Smith/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12877299" aria-describedby="caption-12877299" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877299-492774-450x675.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877299-492774-450x675.jpg" alt="比尔，图为资料照。" width="300" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12877299" class="wp-caption-text">比尔，图为资料照。（Rob Carr/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="12877298" aria-describedby="caption-12877298" style="width: 339px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877298-492771-450x605.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877298-492771-450x605.jpg" alt="米契尔。" width="339" b="455" /></a><figcaption id="caption-12877298" class="wp-caption-text">米契尔。（Jonathan Daniel/Getty Images）</figcaption></figure>
<p>米契尔开赛所向披靡，豪取17分，爵士首节42：33展现王者风范。爵士次节熄火，比尔单节独拿13分，加上威斯特布鲁克和洛培兹（Robin Lopez）点火，半场结束，以70：65逆转爵士。</p>
<p>第三节<ahref="https://github.com/haatdn3351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%87%E6%89%8D.md#1">奇才</a>更变本加厉，板凳球员葛佛（Daniel Gafford）、伯坦斯（Davis Bertans）相挺，将领先扩大到10分。第四节开节威斯特布鲁克连拿4分，史密斯（Ish Smith）及洛培兹（Robin Lopez）合力出击，奇才将伤害扩大到19分。</p>
<p>决胜节米契尔手感发烫，剩下12秒时，攻下单节第19分。爵士追到2分之差，这时比尔与内托（Paul Neto）罚球俱进，让米契尔的分数形同虚设，爵士无奈吞败。<ahref="https://github.com/haatdn3351/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A8%81%E6%96%AF%E5%B8%83%E9%B2%81%E5%85%8B.md#1">威斯布鲁克</a>此役则拿下25分、14篮板与14助攻，缴出大三元。◇</p>
