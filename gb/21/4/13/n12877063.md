<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>山居记事：醉果</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877091-20210413_pineapple_01-537x400.jpg" />
<h6>(图／作者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>古代志怪、神仙幻想小说、故事，频常出现炼丹“七七四十九天”的顺口溜之类的，既无自然周期的意义，也没物化变化的内容，却可赋予读者诸般想像的蜕变，隐寓期待自己的脱胎换骨。</p>
<p>我庭园果树的生命孕育则真实有感多了，何况是活体的晨昏相伴。</p>
<p>自从今年七、八月间，几十种生物分食我一颗株端的<ahref="https://github.com/nvwykf309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E6%A2%A8.md#1">凤梨</a>后，我盯紧另株七月萌长花序的凤梨，看着它从开花到成果到成熟。</p>
<p>并不刻意，不是期待，没有联想，说不出有或没有，我常去单纯看它，甚至它有无长大，我还只是看看它。无念。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12877091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12877091" style="width: 537px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877091-20210413_pineapple_01.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12877091" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877091-20210413_pineapple_01.jpg" alt="凤梨" width="537" b="403" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12877091" class="wp-caption-text">(图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12877096" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12877096" style="width: 533px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877096-20210413_pineapple_02.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12877096" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877096-20210413_pineapple_02.jpg" alt="凤梨" width="533" b="711" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12877096" class="wp-caption-text">(图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12877099" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12877099" style="width: 564px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877099-20210413_pineapple_03.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12877099" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877099-20210413_pineapple_03.jpg" alt="凤梨" width="564" b="878" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12877099" class="wp-caption-text">(图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12877100" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12877100" style="width: 422px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877100-20210413_pineapple_04.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12877100" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877100-20210413_pineapple_04.jpg" alt="书摘" width="422" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12877100" class="wp-caption-text">(图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>二○二○年十一月十一日，这颗<ahref="https://github.com/nvwykf309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%87%A4%E6%A2%A8.md#1">凤梨</a>可以判定开始从下往上微变色泽，而竹鸡群鸣叫声达顶盛。十一月十四日，黄晕渐层上到凤梨的中段；十一月二十二日将近黄红透顶，可能是第一只雌性台湾小稻蝗闻果香而来，直觉联结可以吃了。</p>
<p>十一月二十三日，我终于采下这颗孕育超过一百二十天，也就是一季的凤梨。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12877102" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12877102" style="width: 468px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877102-20210413_pineapple_05.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-12877102" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/04/id12877102-20210413_pineapple_05.jpg" alt="凤梨" width="468" b="855" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12877102" class="wp-caption-text">(图／作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我切开凤梨头尾时，幽微暗香入鼻。削皮、切块后，盛盘拍照，然后吃下凤梨头的一块果肉，喔！一阵阵香酸带甜味浪从舌上震荡传递，跃升为味蕾时空新境界，尾韵果香中还飘荡着花色的娇艳、针刺长叶的狠劲！</p>
<p>这才叫做台湾凤梨！</p>
<p>这颗凤梨我怎忍心一下子吃完，虽然它体积不大。</p>
<p>书写本文的十一月二十六日，我从冰箱再拿出一、二片入口，冰香甜韵中，如同击鼓、鸣金一击，满口味觉的尾音，从音阶回旋，拉上高八度的梁绕。我终于了解昆虫如何醉卧在凤梨果上。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/nvwykf309/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%86%89%E6%9E%9C.md#1">醉果</a>一盘敬邀日月星辰共享。</p>
