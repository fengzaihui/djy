<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>阿马尔菲海岸线美景美食（9）梦乡“波西塔诺”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>（前言）地中海沿岸的南<ahref="https://github.com/ublsmv3253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%84%8F%E5%A4%A7%E5%88%A9.md#1">意大利</a>有一条蜿蜒曲折的海岸线，她的美丽被无数人无数次列入一生必去之50个地方。她就是阿马尔菲海岸（Amalfi Coast），北起那不勒斯湾（Gulf of Naples），南止萨莱诺湾(Gulf of Salerno)，全长50公里。阿马尔菲海岸以其崎岖的地形，陡峭的悬崖，如画的美景，色彩缤纷的房屋，城镇的独特及多样性，被联合国教科文组织列为世界遗产。</p>
<p>阿马尔菲的美是热情的。她的美是要从容的，慢慢的品尝的。那年我与来自4个国家6个城市的9位朋友们从罗马开始，慢行慢看，流连了14天。</p>
<p>上一篇：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/ublsmv3253/djy/blob/master/gb/21/2/8/n12740616.md#1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">阿马尔菲海岸线美景美食（8）美丽的卡普里岛（续）</a></p>
<p>第7天：梦乡般的<ahref="https://github.com/ublsmv3253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E8%A5%BF%E5%A1%94%E8%AF%BA.md#1">波西塔诺</a></p>
<figure id="attachment_12753779" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_02.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753779" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_02-450x337.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">卡普里岛码头。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天我们就要告别美丽的卡普里岛，坐船前往下一站，地中海明珠之一的<ahref="https://github.com/ublsmv3253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%A2%E8%A5%BF%E5%A1%94%E8%AF%BA.md#1">波西塔诺</a>（Positano ）。</p>
<p>波西塔诺获评为“一生中必去的50个地方”之一。一栋栋小巧精致色彩鲜艳的房屋依着山势层层叠叠排在悬崖上，因此得名“垂直的小镇。”</p>
<p>诺贝尔文学奖得主约翰.斯坦贝克在他的游记中这样描述：“波西塔诺令人难以忘怀。他是一个梦乡。你在时，他不很真切；你离开后，他变得栩栩如生。”(“Positano bites deep. It is a dream place that isn’t quite real when you are there and becomes beckoningly real after you have gone.”– John Steinbeck)</p>
<figure id="attachment_12753777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_03.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753777" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_03-450x338.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">离开卡普里岛。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>船到海中，天上飘起了毛毛细雨。等船靠岸，穿着蓝背心的搬运工奔上前来大声问我们要去哪里。别墅的房东事先告诉我搬运工们会把行李直接运到别墅中，我们直接到公车站乘公车上山就行。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12753776" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_04.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753776" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_04-450x600.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波西塔诺公车站。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12753775" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_05.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753775" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_05-450x600.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">鲜花盛开的别墅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>车到半山腰已是阳光灿烂。别墅大门离公车站就几步路，上楼一拐弯就深深的喜欢上这地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12754203" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_06.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12754203" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_06-450x600.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">楼梯拐角。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12754202" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_07.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12754202" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_07-450x552.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="552" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">春茶一壶。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全部的行李已经在楼上等着我们了。先赶紧烧水泡茶。坐在露台上，清茶一杯，面朝大海，春暖花开，何等惬意！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12753774" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_08.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753774" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_08-450x338.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">露台上的风景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12753773" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_09.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753773" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_09-450x557.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="557" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波西塔诺。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>下午茶后大家步行下山，一路看风景，近距离观赏那些漂亮的房屋，然后到镇上找餐厅吃饭，再陪我等落日。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12753772" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_10.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753772" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_10-450x552.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="552" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波西塔诺。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12753771" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_11.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753771" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_11-450x330.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="330" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波西塔诺。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_12753769" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_13.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753769" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_13-450x600.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波西塔诺的晚餐。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还真让我们找到了家相当不错的餐馆，座落在海边。风景好看，饭好吃！</p>
<figure id="attachment_12753768" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_14.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753768" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_14-450x313.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="313" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波西塔诺的黄昏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>步出餐厅，已是黄昏。都说波西塔诺的黄昏与夜色是最美。此时，晚霞漫天，小镇沐浴在暖暖的夕阳下。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12753767" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753767" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_15-450x600.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波西塔诺的黄昏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>慢慢的，最后一抹夕阳离开了圣玛利亚教堂（Chiesa di Santa Maria Assunta）的陶瓷穹顶，夜色开始降临。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12753766" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_16.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753766" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_16-450x543.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="543" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波西塔诺的黄昏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>我快快的跑到下山时看中的一个机位，撑好三脚架，对准镜头，慢慢等待万家灯火的时刻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12753765" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_17.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-12753765" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/02/20210215_amalfi_helen_chou_17-450x296.jpg" alt="行行摄摄看世界, 波西塔诺, 意大利, 阿马尔菲海岸线, 周海伦" width="450" b="296" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波西塔诺的黄昏。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
