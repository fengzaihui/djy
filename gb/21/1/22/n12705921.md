<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【罗厨寻味】羽衣甘蓝豆子汤</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/02b1fe6f198006b17473616e408873ac-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>罗厨制作的这道羽衣甘蓝豆子汤，既有菜香，又有肉香，豆香点缀其中，香气四溢，好味道融合在一起，令人入口难忘。（视频截图）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>羽衣甘蓝含有丰富的维他命A、维他命C、维他命K、叶酸、钙质、铁质及纤维。100克羽衣甘蓝的纤维含量和2个橙子相当，属于高纤食品。纤维有助于肠道蠕动，可帮助消化。</p>
<p>羽衣甘蓝和其它绿叶蔬菜一样，富含抗氧化剂，包括β-胡萝卜素、VC以及各种黄酮和多酚，有保护心脏、降血压、抗炎、抗病毒、抗抑郁和抗癌等效果。</p>
<p>羽衣甘蓝脂肪含量低，被称为低热量高营养的食物，因此也被誉为增肌减脂的超级食物。这种健康蔬菜备受超模、营养师、健身达人的推崇。</p>
<p>鸡肉不仅脂肪含量低，且所含的脂肪多为不饱和脂肪酸，为幼儿、中老年人、心血管疾病患者、病中病后虚弱者理想的蛋白质食品。</p>
<p>鸡的肉质细嫩、滋味鲜美，适合多种烹调方法，并富有营养，有滋补养身的作用。鸡肉不但适于热炒、炖汤，而且是比较适合冷食凉拌的肉类。</p>
<p>罗厨制作的这道<ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E8%A1%A3%E7%94%98%E8%93%9D%E8%B1%86%E5%AD%90%E6%B1%A4.md#1">羽衣甘蓝豆子汤</a><strong>，</strong>将羽衣甘蓝、豆子和鸡丝共同烹煮，既有菜香，又有肉香，豆香点缀其中，香气四溢，好味道融合在一起，令人入口难忘，堪称是寒冷日子里的最佳菜肴。</p>
<h2><strong><ahref="https://github.com/nbjhgz3458/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BE%BD%E8%A1%A3%E7%94%98%E8%93%9D%E8%B1%86%E5%AD%90%E6%B1%A4.md#1">羽衣甘蓝豆子汤</a></strong></h2>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/a9d7d511f20990725bd7d4b180fd17d4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705934" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/a9d7d511f20990725bd7d4b180fd17d4-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>主料：羽衣甘蓝、鸡丝<br />
配料：黄豆<br />
调料：海盐、白糖、胡椒粉、面粉水</p>
<p>做法</p>
<p>1.?将羽衣甘蓝洗干净切碎。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-42.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/1-42-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>2.?将鸡丝炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2-27.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705935" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/2-27-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>3.?放入豆子一起炒香。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/3-22.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705936" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/3-22-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>4.?放入浸鸡胸的原汤。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/4-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705937" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/4-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>5.?加盖慢火煮半小时。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/5-16.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705939" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/5-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>6.?将切好的羽衣甘蓝放入锅内。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/6-12.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705940" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/6-12-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>7.?调入海盐和白糖。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7-8.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705941" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/7-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>8.?放入胡椒粉。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/8-7.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12705942" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/8-7.jpg" alt="" width="412" b="240" /></a></p>
<p>9.?加入半碗面粉水调匀。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/9-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705945" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/9-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
<p>10.?慢慢在锅中搅拌。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10-6.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-12705946" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/01/10-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /></a></p>
