<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>陈文仪：民意是台湾加入CPTPP的坚强后盾</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/04/Chen-speech-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台湾驻加拿大最高代表陈文仪。（任侨生／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年01月08日讯】1月7日，加拿大《财经邮报》刊登台湾驻加拿大代表<ahref="https://github.com/nekroj3812/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E6%96%87%E4%BB%AA.md#1">陈文仪</a>文章，呼吁加拿大政府支持台湾加入<span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">《跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定》（</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">CPTPP</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">）。民调显示，68%加拿大人支持<ahref="https://github.com/nekroj3812/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%8A%A0%E5%85%A5cptpp.md#1">台湾加入CPTPP</a>，文章引用该民调结果称，民意是坚强后盾。</span></span></span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><wbr /><strong>加拿大应支持扩大</strong></span></span><strong><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">CPTPP </span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><wbr />纳入台湾等可靠伙伴</span></span></strong></span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">该文称，亚太</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">15</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">国于</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;">2020</span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">年</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">11</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">月签署《区域全面经济</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">伙</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">伴协定》</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">(<wbr />RCEP)</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">，旨在拓展贸易，减降关税等贸易壁垒。<wbr />该协定的签订象征全球最大自由贸易圈由此诞生，但其仍存在</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">问题</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">及<wbr />间隙。</span></span></span></p>
<div dir="auto">
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><ahref="https://github.com/nekroj3812/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%99%88%E6%96%87%E4%BB%AA.md#1">陈文仪</a>分析，大多数观察家认为</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;">,</span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">《区域全面经济</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">伙</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">伴协定》是</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">以</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">中国</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">为中心</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。加拿大决策者需审</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">慎</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">思</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">考</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">对亚洲贸易的</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">下一步</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">作法。<wbr />加拿大应支持扩大《跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定》（</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">CPTPP</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">），<wbr />纳入台湾等可靠伙伴。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">他说，《跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定》本即具</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">有</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">扩大规模的</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">设计</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">已批准的</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><wbr />7</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">国包括日本、澳洲、墨西哥、新加坡、纽西兰、<wbr />越南，在加拿大在等待智利、秘鲁、汶莱及马来西亚等</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">4</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">国完成国内</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">批</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><wbr />准程序之际，应建立符合其高标准及共识的</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">新成员</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">入会程序架</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">构</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">。<wbr />加拿大可借此展现领导力，与日本等其他成员国研拟共同策略，<wbr />以纳入台湾及英国等有意愿加入国家。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">他说，签署贸易协定的目的并非仅为增</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">加</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">贸易，<wbr />更为强化以规则为基础的市场经济体系。台湾是加拿大全球第</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">13</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><wbr />大</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">及</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">亚洲第</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">5</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">大贸易伙伴，台湾加入《跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定》<wbr />将有助于进一步深化台加</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">既有的</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">紧密经贸关系。</span></span></span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">民调：68%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">加拿大人支持台湾加入《<wbr />跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定》</span></span></span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">该文称，</span></span></span>民调显示，</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">68%</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">加拿大人支持台湾加入《<wbr />跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定》</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;">,?</span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">该民调显示加拿大民众</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">普遍</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">对台<wbr />湾持正面看法。台湾与加拿大同为民主国家，享有建立自由、和平、<wbr />进步及</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">可持</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">续</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">国际</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">秩序的共同目标。台湾与加拿大</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">理应</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">更密切合作，<wbr />加拿大民意是加国政府支持台湾加入《跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定》<wbr />的</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">坚</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">强后盾。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">该文称，</span></span></span>如果台湾加入《跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定》，将是</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">成员国之中</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">仅次于<wbr />日本、加拿大、澳洲及墨西哥的第</span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">5</span></span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">大经济体。<wbr />台湾不仅可作为加拿大产品外销的潜在主要市场，<wbr />也可提供许多目前加拿大需要的其他国家进口的半导体等高科技产品。<wbr />与台湾更紧密合作将为加拿大带来庞大商机，</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">增加</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">其</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">竞争</span></span><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">优势。</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">该文强调，</span></span></span>新冠病毒疫情危机凸显当前全球供应链的脆弱及相互信赖基础对国<wbr />际贸易的重要性。事实证明，威权国家不可靠，<wbr />民主国家亟需共同合作，建立稳固的关键产品供应链。<wbr />作为亚洲最自由经济体之一的台湾，<wbr />与加拿大拥有相似的民主价值观，<wbr />是值得合作且理念相近的可靠伙伴。</span></span></span></p>
<p><strong>支持台湾加入可<span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">协助减缓当前及未来全球贸易挑战</span></span></span></strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: DFKai-SB;"><span style="font-size: medium;">该文称，</span></span></span>加拿大已被迫应对经济胁迫及贸易紧张关系。支持扩大《<wbr />跨太平洋伙伴全面进步协定》纳入台湾参与，<wbr />可协助减缓当前及未来全球贸易挑战的冲击。</span></span></span></p>
