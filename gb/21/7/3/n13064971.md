<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>走进非洲（10）萌企鹅与非洲大陆的尽头</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13065009-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1001-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>非洲大陆的最西南端 - 好望角(Cape of Good Hope)。（图片/周海伦提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>接上文：<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/fdlyci3708/djy/blob/master/gb/21/6/20/n13034327.md#1">走进非洲（9）开普敦的桌山与酒庄</a></p>
<p>司机一早如约接上我们，开始了开普半岛一日游。</p>
<p>我们先去看了看波卡普老区(Bo-Kaap)。这个城市中的小村庄(Village in the city)坐落在市中心西南的信号山(Signal Hill)上，是开普敦保留至今最古老的住宅区。18世纪时，当地的土着人反抗荷兰殖民者，奴隶多从东南亚的马来西亚、印度尼西亚及<ahref="https://github.com/fdlyci3708/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a>其它地区输入。这些奴隶多聚集居住在波卡普，所以这里也被称为“马来区”(Malay Quarter)。老区以色彩缤纷的建筑，狭窄的鹅卵石街道及穆斯林清真寺与美味食物出名。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13064974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064974" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064974-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1002.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13064974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064974-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1002-450x675.jpg" alt="开普敦" width="450" b="675" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064974" class="wp-caption-text">马来区街景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13064975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064975" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064975-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1003.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13064975" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064975-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1003-450x600.jpg" alt="开普敦" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064975" class="wp-caption-text">马来区街景。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>从波卡普出来路经桌山，司机指着云雾缭绕的桌山说：“还是你们运气好，昨天缆车上去一览无遗。今天的天气预报多云，上去也不一定看得清。”</p>
<p>人品好真是没办法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13064977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064977-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1004.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13064977" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064977-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1004-600x328.jpg" alt="桌山, 开普敦" width="600" b="328" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064977" class="wp-caption-text">云雾缭绕的桌山。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>之前我一直以为“桌山国家公园”就是我们昨天缆车上去看到的那几个山头，今天才知道整个国家公园沿着开普半岛连绵不绝的山脊呈南北走势，从北部的信号山，穿过狮头山、桌山、康斯坦蒂亚山、银矿山等，一直到<ahref="https://github.com/fdlyci3708/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%BD%E6%9C%9B%E8%A7%92.md#1">好望角</a>，半岛的75%都在公园内。我们这几天等于是一直在桌山国家公园内看风景。</p>
<p>车子沿着海岸线一路向南，半小时后到了海边小镇豪特湾(Hout Bay)。这是个风景如画的港口小镇。南非语豪特湾的意思是“木头湾”(Wood Bay)，因为十七世纪荷兰人建造开普敦所用的木头基本都来自豪特湾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13064978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064978" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064978-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1005.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13064978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064978-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1005-600x360.jpg" alt="豪特湾, 开普敦" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064978" class="wp-caption-text">豪特湾(Hout Bay)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13064979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064979" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064979-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1006.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13064979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064979-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1006-450x600.jpg" alt="豪特湾" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064979" class="wp-caption-text">豪特湾的躺平族。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在开普敦，只要是有水的地方就有海狮。十有八九还一定会像上面那位老兄那样，四仰八叉的晒日光浴，可谓是躺平族的开山始祖。</p>
<p>从豪特湾再往南不到半小时就是巨砾海滩(Boulders Beach)的<ahref="https://github.com/fdlyci3708/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2.md#1">非洲</a><ahref="https://github.com/fdlyci3708/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%81%E9%B9%85.md#1">企鹅</a>保护区了。企鹅可是我最喜欢的动物了，看着它们一双大脚撑着毛茸茸的身体摇摇摆摆就很治愈对不对？</p>
<figure id="attachment_13064980" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064980" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064980-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1007.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13064980" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064980-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1007-450x600.jpg" alt="企鹅, 非洲" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064980" class="wp-caption-text">保护区的非洲<ahref="https://github.com/fdlyci3708/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BC%81%E9%B9%85.md#1">企鹅</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据说现在保护区内有3千多只企鹅。游人们可以通过宽宽的木头步道一直走到海边，近距离观看企鹅。很多企鹅直接在海边沙滩上挖个洞孵蛋，也有不少在离沙滩远一些的灌木丛下筑窝。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13064981" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064981" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064981-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1008.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13064981" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064981-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1008-600x452.jpg" alt="企鹅" width="600" b="452" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064981" class="wp-caption-text">灌木丛里的企鹅蛋。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13064982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064982" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064982-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1009.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13064982" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064982-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1009-450x676.jpg" alt="企鹅" width="450" b="676" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064982" class="wp-caption-text">憨憨的企鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近距离观察才知道，非洲企鹅的眼部居然是粉色的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13064983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064983" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064983-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1010.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13064983" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064983-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1010-600x899.jpg" alt="企鹅" width="600" b="899" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064983" class="wp-caption-text">企鹅妈妈身后毛茸茸的企鹅宝宝。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13064984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064984" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064984-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1011.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13064984" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064984-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1011-600x400.jpg" alt="企鹅" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064984" class="wp-caption-text">嬉水的企鹅。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13064985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064985" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064985-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1012.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13064985" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064985-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1012-600x401.jpg" alt="企鹅" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064985" class="wp-caption-text">恩爱的两公婆。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>企鹅也是动物界夫唱妇随、从一而终的楷模喔。 两公婆轮流孵化喂养小宝宝，终身厮守。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13064986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064986" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064986-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1013.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13064986" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064986-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1013-600x404.jpg" alt="好望角望角" width="600" b="404" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064986" class="wp-caption-text">进入<ahref="https://github.com/fdlyci3708/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A5%BD%E6%9C%9B%E8%A7%92.md#1">好望角</a>。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再往南，就到了开普半岛的尽头了，也就是非洲大陆最西南的好望角。在苏伊士运河未开通前，好望角是欧洲通往东方的海路必经之地。哪怕到了今天，特大油轮若无法进入苏伊士运河，仍需绕行好望角。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13064990" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064990" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064990-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1014.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13064990" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064990-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1014-600x450.jpg" alt="好望角好望角" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064990" class="wp-caption-text">居然找不到那张带有好望角标志的照片了，只好网上找了一张。（By <span style="color: blue;"><ahref=" https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=73453739 " target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Chs87</a></span> CC BY-SA 4.0）</figcaption></figure>
<p>很多人以为好望角就是非洲大陆的最南端了，其实不是。好望角是最西南端。距离好望角东南方约150公里的厄加勒斯角(Cape Agulhas)才是真正的非洲最南端。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13064994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064994" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064994-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1015.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13064994" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064994-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1015-450x600.jpg" alt="好望角" width="450" b="600" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064994" class="wp-caption-text">好望角的指路石(Cairn rocks)。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13064995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13064995" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064995-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1016.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-13064995" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13064995-20210703_africa_helen_chou_1016-600x304.jpg" alt="好望角" width="600" b="304" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13064995" class="wp-caption-text">好望角的风光。（图片/周海伦提供）</figcaption></figure>

<p>领略了好望角的风光，我们选择从内陆开回开普敦。远远望去，桌山依旧笼罩在云雾中。难道神仙们还在山上进餐？</p>
