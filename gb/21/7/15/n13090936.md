<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>龙卷风袭击后 美警长和副手瓦砾中折叠国旗</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090960-Sergeant-Derek-Bogenschutz-and-Deputy-Eric-Smitley-WEB-American-flag-found-in-tornado-debris-2-700x420-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>警长德里克·博根舒茨（Derek Bogenschutz，左）和另外一名警察埃里克·史密特利（Eric Smitley）折叠在废墟中发现的美国国旗。（由杰县警长办公室提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年07月15日讯】（英文大纪元记者Michael Wing报导／刘谨信编译）在被<ahref="https://github.com/eekhrg303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%8D%B7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">龙卷风</a>肆虐过的社区中，一面美国星条旗从旗杆掉落下来，与房屋的废墟混在一起。如果你看到这样的场景，将会怎么做？最近，两名印第安纳州警察的举动感动了无数人的心。</p>
<p>2021年6月15日，美国国家气象服务中心（NWS）预报在印第安纳的杰县（Jay County）将有风暴。很快，风暴伴随<ahref="https://github.com/eekhrg303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BE%99%E5%8D%B7%E9%A3%8E.md#1">龙卷风</a>到来了。当一切归于平静，当地社区已经遭到严重破坏，电力设施和通讯塔台被损坏，很多居民的住宅被毁于一旦。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13090969" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13090969" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090969-Sergeant-Derek-Bogenschutz-and-Deputy-Eric-Smitley-folded-American-flag-found-in-tornado-debris-5-600x357.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13090969" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090969-Sergeant-Derek-Bogenschutz-and-Deputy-Eric-Smitley-folded-American-flag-found-in-tornado-debris-5-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13090969" class="wp-caption-text">当时在杰县的龙卷风造成的部分破坏。（由杰县警长办公室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>数名警察随后被派遣来到受损严重的居民区，经逐一检查，确信所有居民安全后，警长德里克?博根舒茨（Derek Bogenschutz）发现一面美国<ahref="https://github.com/eekhrg303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%97%97.md#1">国旗</a>被掩盖在废墟中，德里克和另外一名警察埃里克?史密特利（Eric Smitley）把这面国旗从瓦砾中捡起，并仔细地折叠，收好。</p>
<p>博根舒茨表示，他的父亲曾加入美国陆军，他从父亲那里学到正确折叠<ahref="https://github.com/eekhrg303/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E6%97%97.md#1">国旗</a>的方法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13090974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13090974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090974-Sergeant-Derek-Bogenschutz-and-Deputy-Eric-Smitley-folded-American-flag-found-in-tornado-debris-3-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13090974" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090974-Sergeant-Derek-Bogenschutz-and-Deputy-Eric-Smitley-folded-American-flag-found-in-tornado-debris-3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13090974" class="wp-caption-text">一张照片记录了风暴期间发生的破坏。（由杰县警长办公室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13090976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13090976" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090976-Sergeant-Derek-Bogenschutz-and-Deputy-Eric-Smitley-folded-American-flag-found-in-tornado-debris-4-1-600x415.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-large wp-image-13090976" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090976-Sergeant-Derek-Bogenschutz-and-Deputy-Eric-Smitley-folded-American-flag-found-in-tornado-debris-4-1-600x415.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13090976" class="wp-caption-text">当时袭击印第安纳州杰县的风暴造成的破坏。（由杰县警长办公室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外一名负责记录此次灾难的警察，为他们拍下了这些照片，并上传到社交媒体。两名警察立即吸引了大量网民的注意，美国著名电视节目“福克斯和朋友”还对他们进行了采访。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13090978" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13090978" style="width: 448px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090978-Sgt.-Derek-Bogenschutz-450x562.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13090978" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090978-Sgt.-Derek-Bogenschutz-450x562.jpg" alt="" width="448" b="560" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13090978" class="wp-caption-text">德里克·博根舒茨（Derek Bogenschutz）。（由杰县警长办公室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13090979" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13090979" style="width: 455px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090979-Deputy-Eric-Smitley-600x576.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class=" wp-image-13090979" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/07/id13090979-Deputy-Eric-Smitley-600x576.jpg" alt="" width="455" b="437" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13090979" class="wp-caption-text">埃里克·史密特利（Eric Smitley）。（杰县治安官办公室提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当被问及为何这样对待国旗时，史密特利说：“我只是觉得应该这么做，这是对国旗的尊重，也是对我们国家的尊重。”博根舒茨补充道：“（在这次救灾过程中）社区居民给了我们数不清的支持，他们互相帮助，尽自己所能走出门做义工。”</p>
