<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>住宅外观改造 12种色彩搭配让旧屋焕然一新</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1253529022-600x400.jpg" />
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2021年03月21日讯】(大纪元记者郭静馨编译报导)对于<ahref="https://github.com/jfwhtu349/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%8F%E5%AE%85.md#1">住宅</a>外观的设计，外墙涂料颜色一直是大家讨论最热烈的主题之一。颜色扮演着关键的角色，决定住家设计品味的第一印象。不论是想突显住宅的建筑细节，或者简单利用百叶窗与装饰板衬托建筑外观，都必须谨慎选择颜色。住宅外观的色彩通常是由外墙、装饰板、大门、窗户等整体的颜色配合而成。</p>
<p>以2021年<ahref="https://github.com/jfwhtu349/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E4%BD%8F%E5%AE%85.md#1">住宅</a>外观设计为例，主要是使用对比强烈的材料、材质和颜色，不同于往年都是使用相同材料，设计作品的呈现多数是金属和木头搭配大地色调。如果你需要激发<ahref="https://github.com/jfwhtu349/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%8D%E8%89%B2.md#1">配色</a>灵感，不妨以住家的室内规模和装潢为蓝本，再参考邻居的住宅建筑风格，以及四周的自然环境。专家认为，最佳的建筑外观色彩是与环境融为一体。以下建议12种高雅、美丽的外观配色，赋予您的住宅全新的风貌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12823481" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_323721014-e1616296445502.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823481" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_323721014-600x400.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">淡灰色装饰板衬托屋顶、窗户与大门，显得更立体。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>★</strong> 米灰色(Revere Pewter)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821921" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/04-1-e1616144169460.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821921" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/04-1-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">米灰色的<ahref="https://github.com/jfwhtu349/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%85%8D%E8%89%B2.md#1">配色</a>建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>暖灰色或米灰色已经使用了很长一段时间，今年终于跃上受欢迎的色彩名单。暖灰是百搭色系，经常用于室内深灰或蓝色墙面的装饰板，而作为英式传统乡村住宅砖砌外墙的颜色，更能达到令人惊喜的效果，高雅内敛，却又舒适温暖。它的颜色深度能够压住其它色调，堪当主色调，同时又非常明亮，能够衬托各种色系，作为辅助的角色。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>★</strong> 休止灰(Repose Grey)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821922" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/05-1-e1616144183953.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821922" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/05-1-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">休止灰的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>古铜色金属窗框和大门，以及深棕色核桃木廊柱，似乎在中性的休止灰木墙板的调和下，更有安定感，不至于被周围环境的色彩所淹没。休止灰目前在网路上非常受欢迎，如果考虑选用明亮的中性灰色系，那么休止灰是不二之选，它与深棕色产生鲜明的对比。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12823484" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_507468190-e1616296119749.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823484" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_507468190-600x401.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="401" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">纯白外墙。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12823479" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_191273030-e1616297087762.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823479" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_191273030-600x445.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="445" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">米色墙面、白色装饰板与石材柱墩。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>★</strong> 冰蓝白(Ice Mist)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821918" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/01-6-e1616144114235.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/01-6-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">冰蓝白的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>中性色如白色，就不只是单一色调的白色，有好多种白色可以选择，比如亮蓝色的大门、前廊粉蓝色(powder-blue)户外桌椅和粉蓝色吊灯，此时外墙可以搭配以蓝色为基底的冰蓝白，透着隐隐约约的淡蓝。每种色彩都有其独特性，但各自的特色却搭配得如此和谐。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>★</strong> 雪花石膏白(Alabaster)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821919" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/02-6-e1616144143819.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821919" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/02-6-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">雪花石膏白的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>有些人特别喜欢各种不同色调的白如米白、灰白、珍珠白、月光白等等，同时对于传统的米白色住宅有着浓厚的怀旧情感，那么可以考虑选用雪花石膏白的外墙，雪白色调中微妙透着黄色的底色，非常适合新旧混搭的现代住宅外观，包括舒适的现代农舍、以灰泥(stucco)包覆外墙的滨海住宅等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12823487" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_662207830-e1616296609775.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823487" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_662207830-600x400.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">红色系的木梁搭配暖色系外墙，特别突出。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12823486" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_557489671-e1616296944554.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823486" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_557489671-600x400.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">低斜度屋顶、棕色墙板、黑色门框和玻璃车库门。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;">★ 多米诺黑色(Domino)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821920" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-3-e1616144156738.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821920" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/03-3-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">多米诺黑色的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在完全是中性色系的调色盘里，点缀些许黑色，看起来特别优雅而自信，也增添趣味性。将大门与百叶窗粉刷成黑色，让米色的乙烯基仿真木外墙(vinyl siding)显得细致、立体、层次更丰富。再搭配白色的屋顶装饰板、门框、窗框、木栏杆等，整体感觉简洁、清爽，跳脱单一色调。</p>
<h2></h2>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>★</strong> 木炭灰(Kendall Charcoal)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821923" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-e1616144198190.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821923" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/06-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">肯德尔木炭灰的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>肯德尔木炭灰似乎能包容各种元素，搭配性相当强，展现现代建筑个性风。中性的深灰色外墙既能带出染色木墙板的温暖质感，再搭配黑色金属门框与窗框、以及门廊前的绿色盆栽，各种元素巧妙地融合在一起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12823496" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1705820911-e1616296520796.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823496" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1705820911-600x566.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="566" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">古典风格的住宅，白色前廊与廊柱、装饰板衬托亮丽的绿松石色大门。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12823493" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1119541529-e1616296810251.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823493" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1119541529-600x385.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="385" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">乡村住宅，砖造屋顶与外墙，搭配紫色大门与百叶窗。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>★</strong> 墨西卡丽绿松石色(Mexicali Turquoise)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821924" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/07-e1616144209113.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821924" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/07-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">墨西卡丽绿松石色的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>有些住宅外观的色彩搭配完全颠覆了传统，例如传统的美国殖民式住宅建筑外观，通常只使用单一色调，而且都是白色的，然而在白色的古典基调中，还是可以加入其它的色调，展现现代感，最简单有效的方式就是利用百叶窗和大门的配色，为传统住宅创造现代风貌。一般配色不会同时使用薰衣草灰与蓝绿色，然而朦胧的灰紫色让百叶窗显得更精致，蓝绿色大门也格外引人注目，让人不禁想伫足观赏。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>★</strong> 埃利灰(Ellie Gray)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/08-e1616144223702.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821925" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/08-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">埃利灰的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你钟情于具有现代感的灰色系，由于灰色的本质就稍带着金属般的冷调、严肃，不妨在这理性、冷静的色调中加入些许温暖的元素，例如搭配明亮的橘色系，或者使用对比强烈的材质如木头，尤其是染色的木墙板，由浅色到深色。在这种现代感的配色中，灰色扮演着不可或缺的平衡角色。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12823480" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_230693668-e1616296686858.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823480" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_230693668-600x400.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">石材廊柱与木质大门。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12823492" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1112153720-e1616296387155.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823492" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1112153720-600x363.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">棕色木纹墙板连结白色外墙与黑色车库门。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;">★ 半透明木材涂装(Cedar Naturaltone Transparent Wood Finish)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821926" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/09-e1616144235749.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821926" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/09-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">染色木材的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>木材染料的颜色种类很多，绝对不会比传统涂料的颜色少。每种颜色的染料都能让住宅外观如添新衣，焕然一新。尤其是中间色调的木材染料可以搭配各种风格的住宅，从古典到现代，从乡村农舍到别墅。木材染料就跟<ahref="https://github.com/jfwhtu349/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B2%B9%E6%BC%86.md#1">油漆</a>一样，必须先在外墙样板上试涂，观察一天，看看最后呈现的颜色是否符合你想要的色泽。最万无一失的主色调是白色，多数古典的住宅，常将窗户的装饰板、大门、廊柱涂上白色。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;">★ 知更鸟蛋蓝(Robin egg blue)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821927" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/10-5-e1616144250359.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821927" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/10-5-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">知更鸟蛋蓝的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>乡村风格的住宅在外观的色彩与样式搭配上比较自由、随性，没有太多规范。按照传统的做法，通常正面外墙的所有元素会统一使用一种颜色，在此不妨跳脱常规，灰绿色外墙搭配天然原木山形屋顶，在灰色调的色彩中加入温度，然后大门涂上明亮的知更鸟蛋蓝，强烈对比绿灰色墙面，利用白色的大门装饰板、窗框、廊柱与栏杆，将原木色、绿灰色和知更鸟蛋蓝结合在一起。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12823489" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1017132577-e1616297040340.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12823489" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/shutterstock_1017132577-600x363.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="363" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">灰色屋顶、白色墙面，搭配木纹外墙，小木屋风格简洁、不失现代感。(图/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>★</strong> 椰浆白(Coconut Twist)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821928" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/11-6-e1616144268283.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821928" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/11-6-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">椰浆白的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>住宅外观的材质经常是色彩规划的基础，例如砖造外墙自然就散发出温暖的红色调，此时砖墙便成为主色，再搭配其它颜色来衬托砖墙。而牧场风格的住宅(ranch house)，以暖黄色系的石灰岩为外墙，与住宅建筑和自然景观融为一体。为了保持低调、简约的风格，木百叶和窗户都刷上中性的黄色系，几乎与石灰岩外墙的颜色相同，再搭配暖色的木质大门。</p>
<h2><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>★</strong> 细沙白(Crumb Cookie)</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_12821929" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/12-e1616144282258.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12821929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/03/12-600x143.jpg" alt="exterior colors" width="600" b="143" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">细沙白的配色建议。(图/Edward Kuo)</figcaption></figure>
<p>白色灰泥外墙与白色百叶窗，简洁、干净的住宅外观，同色系搭配呈现简单的风格。带有细沙质感的白色墙面突显前门上方米黄色系石材外墙的温暖、天然、朴实，同时石墙也将人们的视线引向棕色木质大门。屋顶则是石材的原色。营造整体一致的外观风格，但不失简单、明快。</p>
