<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>郑文灿视察国中教育会考试场 考生权益不打折</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12951410-497998-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>郑文灿市长视察国中教育会考试场防疫情形，疫情期间考生权益不打折扣。（桃园市府新闻处提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2021年05月15日讯】（大纪元记者徐乃义台湾桃园报导）桃园市长<ahref="https://github.com/boohxn302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>15日上午前往桃园高中，视察<ahref="https://github.com/boohxn302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E4%B8%AD%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2%E4%BC%9A%E8%80%83.md#1">国中教育会考</a><ahref="https://github.com/boohxn302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%95%E5%9C%BA.md#1">试场</a>防疫情形，郑文灿表示，文昌、慈文、大有三所国中的学生在桃园高中应考，会场启用7个备用试场，不仅考场秩序良好、备用考场相当完备，学校也准备充足的防疫物资让考生安心应试，若考生因检疫或隔离事由无法到场应考，将得参加5/29-30的补行考试，考生应试及升学权益不受影响。市府将依规定进行检查与管制，疫情期间<ahref="https://github.com/boohxn302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%80%83%E7%94%9F%E6%9D%83%E7%9B%8A.md#1">考生权益</a>不打折扣，让考生应试顺利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_12951412" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12951412" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12951412-498008.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12951412" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12951412-498008-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12951412" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/boohxn302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%83%91%E6%96%87%E7%81%BF.md#1">郑文灿</a>市长与学生志工互动。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_12951411" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-12951411" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12951411-498007.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-12951411" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/05/id12951411-498007-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-12951411" class="wp-caption-text">单一入口进场及考场规则。（桃园市府新闻处提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>郑文灿指出，15日是<ahref="https://github.com/boohxn302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9B%BD%E4%B8%AD%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2%E4%BC%9A%E8%80%83.md#1">国中教育会考</a>的第一天，桃园共有2万1019名考生分别在19处考场应考，各考场皆备有7个备用<ahref="https://github.com/boohxn302/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%AF%95%E5%9C%BA.md#1">试场</a>提供特殊考生使用，今（2021）年因疫情取消家长陪考，各国中也设置考生服务区及考生服务队，由学校老师提供考生应考协助，服务相当周到。◇</p>
