<p><strong>作法</strong></p>
<p><strong>1. 打发蛋黄</strong> 搅拌盆里的油脂还有水分要先擦拭干净后，再放入蛋黄、蜂蜜、细砂糖(A)，以快速打发至泛白后转至中速打至全发。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;">打发前先进行“温蛋”。也就是把冰箱取出的蛋黄、蛋白，以隔水加热的方式到40度，再进行打发。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13378953" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378953" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378953-P.134-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378953" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378953-P.134-1-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378953" class="wp-caption-text">打发蛋黄。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>2. 加入油脂</strong> 蛋黄打发完后，把无盐奶油、鲜奶、榛果酱、蜂蜜加热到沸腾后冲入黑巧克力中拌匀，直到巧克力溶解，然后加入打发的蛋黄糊中。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378954" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378954-P.134-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378954-P.134-2-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378954" class="wp-caption-text">加入油脂。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>3. 蛋白打到湿性发泡?</strong>另取一个搅拌盆，搅拌盆里的油脂还有水分要先擦拭干净后放入蛋白、柠檬汁，打发至发泡后，加入1／3的细砂糖(B)，以快速打发至有纹路后再分两次把细砂糖加入并打发至湿性泡。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;">也就是拉起时呈现鸟嘴状。蛋白中加入砂糖打发后的气泡会更为细密且安定性高，不过加入砂糖时最好要分成2-3次，边加入边打发，避免一次将砂糖倒入会导致蛋白浓度过高造成打发失败。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13378956" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378956" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378956-P.134-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378956" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378956-P.134-3-450x344.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378956" class="wp-caption-text">蛋白打到湿性发泡。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>4. 混合面糊与蛋白</strong> 把已经打发的蛋白跟前面的蛋黄巧克力面糊一起做混合。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378959" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378959" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378959-P.134-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378959" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378959-P.134-4-450x325.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="325" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378959" class="wp-caption-text">混合面糊与蛋白。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>5. 面糊倒入烤盘中?</strong>将面糊倒入已经铺好烘焙纸的烤盘中，将表面抹平后把空气敲掉。将烤箱预热后，以上火200℃，下火150℃烘烤10分钟，将烤盘掉头后改成上下火150℃烘烤3分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378961" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378961-P.135-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378961-P.135-1-450x318.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="318" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378961" class="wp-caption-text">面糊倒入烤盘中。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6. 制作咖啡酒糖液</strong> 烘烤的时间可以制作咖啡酒糖液。准备好制作咖啡酒糖液的材料，包括细砂糖、水、浓缩咖啡、君度橙酒煮一起拌匀煮热，等冷却就可以使用，做好后会用毛刷沾刷在蛋糕表面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378965" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378965" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378965-P.135-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378965" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378965-P.135-2-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378965" class="wp-caption-text">制作咖啡酒糖液。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>7. 制作澄酒香堤</strong> 把材料中的动物性鲜奶油（A）打发，加入融化黑巧克力、君度橙酒、动物性鲜奶油（B）一起拌匀后即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378966" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378966" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378966-P.135-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378966" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378966-P.135-3-450x326.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378966" class="wp-caption-text">制作澄酒香堤。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>8. 沾上咖啡酒糖液</strong> 台面上铺上白报纸将烤好的蛋糕体倒扣，去除烘焙纸，把咖啡酒糖液沾刷在蛋糕上面，只要有沾附到的程度就可以。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;">因为这个蛋糕是要做成卷，如果吸收了太多酒糖液，蛋糕体就会太软烂，进行卷的时候会崩掉或裂掉。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13378968" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378968" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378968-P.135-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378968" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378968-P.135-4-450x344.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378968" class="wp-caption-text">沾上咖啡酒糖液。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>9. 抹馅?</strong>将澄酒香堤均匀的抹在蛋糕体上，抹平后进行卷制，把擀面棍慢慢往前移动，让蛋糕卷收口朝下静置等待蛋糕定型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378970" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378970-P.136-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378970-P.136-1-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378970" class="wp-caption-text">抹馅。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>10. 进行装饰</strong> 蛋糕卷表面用锯齿状的挤花嘴平均的挤上鲜奶油巧克力香堤，让它出现条纹状，在蛋糕卷的前后贴上雪花巧克力片，然后在上面放上新鲜覆盆子、圈卷状巧克力<sup>?</sup>、山萝卜叶、焦糖爆米花碎块，一起做点缀即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378971" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378971" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378971-P.136-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378971" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378971-P.136-2-450x317.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="317" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378971" class="wp-caption-text">进行装饰。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>主厨的实作笔记</strong><strong> Notes</strong></span></h2>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/vzozzr376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%B4%E8%96%AA.md#1">柴薪</a>是树干的意思所以我们要做出树干的纹路。</p>
<p>台湾每年都有举办圣诞<ahref="https://github.com/vzozzr376/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9F%B4%E8%96%AA.md#1">柴薪</a>蛋糕的竞赛，当然我也指导过竞赛的蛋糕设计制作，并获得奖项，将这款美味的柴薪蛋糕重现出来分享给大家。</p>
<p>这款蛋糕他主要的味道就是巧克力跟橙酒，也是冬天圣诞节天气寒冷时吃的，所以他的酒味会比较重一点。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">?圈卷状的巧克力</span></h3>
<p>制作圈卷状的巧克力是以调温巧克力来进行，作法是将切成细长形的慕斯围边胶片放在大理石板上，以平放的方向固定好，并在胶片最上方倒上适量的调温过的巧克力，以刮板朝由左往右薄薄推展开来。</p>
