<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>作法</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>1.打发蛋黄筛入粉料</strong> 搅拌盆里的油脂还有水分要先擦拭干净<sup>?</sup>，再放入蛋黄、细砂糖(A)打发，加入过筛的低筋面粉、玉米粉拌匀。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;">低筋面粉使用前最好要筛过两次。过筛第一次时可以把杂质与结块筛除成为细微粉末后，再筛一次。而过筛时，最好把低筋面粉与玉米粉直接筛在烘焙纸上。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13378901" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378901" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378901-P.46-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378901" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378901-P.46-1-450x335.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="335" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378901" class="wp-caption-text">打发蛋黄筛入粉料。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. <strong>蛋白打至湿性发泡</strong> 另取一个搅拌盆，放入蛋白，打发至发泡后，加入1/3的砂糖，以快速打发至有纹路，再分两次各加入1/3的砂糖打发至湿性发泡，也就是拉起时呈现鸟嘴状。打发的蛋白分两次加入步骤1中拌匀。</p>
<p><span style="color: #993300;">面糊只要拌匀就好，混拌过头会消泡烤出来的蛋糕膨松不佳，入口干硬。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_13378903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378903" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378903-P.46-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378903" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378903-P.46-2-450x316.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378903" class="wp-caption-text">蛋白打至湿性发泡。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. <strong>面糊倒入烤盘烘烤</strong> 烤箱预热好。在烤盘上铺入烘焙纸，倒入面糊后抹平，并把空气敲掉，以上火200℃，下火150℃烘烤10分钟，将烤盘掉头后改成上下火150℃烘烤3分钟，取出后，铺上白报纸将烤好的蛋糕体倒扣，去除烘焙纸，烘烤的时间可以制作栗子卡士达酱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378904" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378904" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378904-P.46-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378904" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378904-P.46-3-450x344.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378904" class="wp-caption-text">面糊倒入烤盘烘烤。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.<strong> 制作栗子馅</strong> 将有糖栗子泥、无糖栗子泥一起拌匀，再加入动物性鲜奶油一起搅拌均匀。冷藏备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378906" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378906" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378906-P.46-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378906" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378906-P.46-4-450x324.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="324" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378906" class="wp-caption-text">制作栗子馅。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. <strong>制作栗子卡士达</strong> 将蛋黄、细砂糖、玉米粉拌匀，鲜奶、香草荚煮到沸腾后过筛再冲入收稠，加入无糖栗子泥拌匀。再将动物性鲜奶油、细砂糖打发做成香缇备用??。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378909" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378909-P.47-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378909" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378909-P.47-1-450x314.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="314" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378909" class="wp-caption-text">制作栗子卡士达。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>6. 挤上内馅</strong> 台面上铺上白报纸将烤好的蛋糕体倒扣，去除烘焙纸，抹上鲜奶油，挤上栗子馅与栗子卡士达、放上糖渍栗子碎后进行卷制。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378915" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378915-P.47-2.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378915" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378915-P.47-2-450x332.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="332" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378915" class="wp-caption-text">挤上内馅。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378911-P.47-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-13378911" alt="" /></a><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378911-P.47-2.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-13378911" alt="" /></a><strong>7. 卷蛋糕</strong> 将擀面棍放在白纸下方靠近自己的一侧。先用一小段白报纸把擀面棍包起来并压紧，再与蛋糕一侧一起上提往前下压，慢慢把擀面棍往前移动，同时要把擀面棍往自己的方向卷，用尺放在擀面棍的位置固定，让蛋糕卷收口朝下静置等待定型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378917" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378917-P.47-3.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378917" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378917-P.47-3-450x322.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="322" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378917" class="wp-caption-text">卷蛋糕。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>8. 装饰</strong> 蛋糕定型，切成等量大小，表面挤上栗子馅，再放香缇、整颗的糖渍栗子，最后以圈卷状的<ahref="https://github.com/obavqw396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B7%A7%E5%85%8B%E5%8A%9B.md#1">巧克力</a><sup>???</sup>与少许金箔做装饰，撒上糖粉即完成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13378918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13378918" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378918-P.47-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13378918" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/11/id13378918-P.47-4-450x341.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="341" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13378918" class="wp-caption-text">装饰。（台湾广厦提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>主厨的实作笔记</strong><strong> Notes</strong></span></h2>
<p>初到法国时，第一个寻找的甜点就是<ahref="https://github.com/obavqw396/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%92%99%E5%B8%83%E6%9C%97.md#1">蒙布朗</a>，吃一口就让味觉感到惊讶，栗子味很浓，蛋糕整体很甜，冲击力很强，甜味迅速化开，最后留下栗子的香气，原来正统的蒙布朗是这样粗旷、直接单纯的甜点。所以后来萌生将栗子馅夹入生乳卷的概念，将栗子的味道变得细致些，搭配蛋糕及鲜奶油，整体口感风味也变得更柔软与柔和。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">?搅拌盆里的油脂或水分要擦拭干净</span></h3>
<p>所使用的搅拌盆或打蛋器，如果沾有油脂，蛋白就会无法打发，因此使用前一定要洗干净。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">??蛋白要分两次加入</span></h3>
<p>蛋白中加入砂糖打发后的气泡会更为细密且安定性高，不过加入砂糖时最好要分成2-3次，边加入边打发，避免一次将砂糖倒入会导致蛋白浓度过高而造成打发失败。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">???圈卷状的巧克力</span></h3>
<p>制作圈卷状的巧克力是以调温巧克力来进行，作法是将切成细长形的慕斯围边胶片放在大理石板上，以平放的方向固定好，并在胶片最上方倒上适量的调温过的巧克力，以刮板朝由左往右薄薄推展开来。</p>
<p>再用齿梳划出线条，以刮板刮除外侧多余的巧克力，迅速的从大理石板把慕斯围边胶片取下、围绕扭转放在擀面棍上，两端加以固定，等凝固后即可将胶片拆除。</p>
