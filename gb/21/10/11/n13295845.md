<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>让人心花朵朵开的中式小点心：油炸荷花酥</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13313146-709b2d8d306f7a160711e1782e08ca44-1-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>荷花酥。（采实文化提供）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>层层绽开的花瓣酥皮，搭配探出头来的咸蛋黄莲蓉馅，咸甜滋味恰到好处。各位失宠的小主还不赶快学起来，用这道层次美丽的宫廷<ahref="https://github.com/ubbrbk395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%B7%E8%8A%B1%E9%85%A5.md#1">荷花酥</a>，让皇上心花朵朵开，就能成功逃出冷宫啦！</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>示范影片</strong></span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_13295961" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295961" style="width: 164px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295961-dafd750b272aab48cbf8a205fcd1048c.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-13295961" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295961-dafd750b272aab48cbf8a205fcd1048c.png" alt="" width="164" b="164" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295961" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/ubbrbk395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8D%B7%E8%8A%B1%E9%85%A5.md#1">荷花酥</a>。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>材料（约可做出</strong><strong>10</strong><strong>颗）</strong></span></h2>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【<ahref="https://github.com/ubbrbk395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E9%BB%84%E9%A6%85.md#1">蛋黄馅</a>】</strong></span></h3>
<p>咸蛋黄3颗</p>
<p>料理米酒少许</p>
<p>椰丝50g</p>
<p>糖粉20g</p>
<p>无盐奶油20g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【油酥】</strong></span></h3>
<p>低筋面粉 100g</p>
<p>猪油 35g</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>【水油皮】</strong></span></h3>
<p>低筋面粉 110g</p>
<p>猪油 10g</p>
<p>二号砂糖 15g</p>
<p>冰水 55g</p>
<p>粉红色色素 1滴（或红麹粉2g）</p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;"><strong>制作内馅</strong></span></h2>
<p>1在咸蛋黄表面涂上米酒，放入预热的烤箱，以上下火180度烘烤10分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295849" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295849-509347de70c6341b97149f6768f46892.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295849-509347de70c6341b97149f6768f46892-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295849" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥1。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.烤好的咸蛋黄放上烘焙纸包起，用擀面棍碾碎至没有颗粒。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295851" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295851" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295851-7defb32db19bf119ff227914f76898ec.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295851" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295851-7defb32db19bf119ff227914f76898ec-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295851" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥2。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.碎蛋黄倒入盆中，稍微拌开来，再倒入糖粉拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295852" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295852" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295852-8a4500b44fdb153faf8a0edb2a0f736b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295852" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295852-8a4500b44fdb153faf8a0edb2a0f736b-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295852" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥3。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.加入无盐奶油拌匀，略呈稠状后，再加入椰丝拌匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295854" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295854" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295854-0bf623a208b70d1e51d6fe0169a7c376.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295854" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295854-0bf623a208b70d1e51d6fe0169a7c376-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295854" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥4。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13295855" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295855" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295855-67a68e149daa07d35eb751f014dc2d78.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295855" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295855-67a68e149daa07d35eb751f014dc2d78-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295855" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥5。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.将<ahref="https://github.com/ubbrbk395/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9B%8B%E9%BB%84%E9%A6%85.md#1">蛋黄馅</a>搓成10颗约15g的球形内馅，放入冷冻成形。</p>
<p>Tips：用手指稍微施力将内馅捏成扎实的球形。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295857" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295857-89640be5b34b8aadf5240b37a13f7909.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295857" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295857-89640be5b34b8aadf5240b37a13f7909-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295857" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥6。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>制作油酥</strong></span></h3>
<p>6.过筛后的低筋面粉挖出一个小洞，放入猪油，将面粉从外向内覆盖，让猪油均匀混合面粉，推匀成团后，包上保鲜膜冷藏15分钟。</p>
<p>Tips：也可使用奶油制作，但起酥可能不会开得那么漂亮。此阶段留意不要过度搅拌。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295859" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295859" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295859-27d80336f5c4bf5b832591db5b1cb99c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295859" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295859-27d80336f5c4bf5b832591db5b1cb99c-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295859" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥7。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13295861" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295861" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295861-83a3b3609bf7b57fbc97e2fb0bd83bd1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295861" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295861-83a3b3609bf7b57fbc97e2fb0bd83bd1-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295861" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥8。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>制作水油皮</strong></span></h3>
<p>7.过筛的低筋面粉中加入猪油、砂糖；冰水加入粉红色食用色素搅散，倒入盆中混合。</p>
<p>Tips：猪油会因温度融化，务必使用冰水，才易成形；倒入冰水时，要同时持续搅拌，才能混合均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295862" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295862" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295862-d185649f5e57cc2975ab7a2bf507f332.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295862" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295862-d185649f5e57cc2975ab7a2bf507f332-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295862" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥9。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.将水油皮捏成团后稍微拍平，用保鲜膜包起，冷藏15分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295864" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295864" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295864-5683b7c134288bee40bdef68fd0a28d7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295864-5683b7c134288bee40bdef68fd0a28d7-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295864" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥10。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9冷藏后的油酥与水油皮各分成10等份，搓成圆球形。</p>
<p>Tips：还没用到的油酥跟水油皮记得用保鲜膜盖起，避免水分流失表面干裂。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295869" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295869" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295869-69791357444e39ecc5151d6912d463e9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295869-69791357444e39ecc5151d6912d463e9-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295869" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥13。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>油皮包油酥</strong></span></h3>
<p>10.取一份油酥跟水油皮。擀面棍稍微撒上手粉，将水油皮面团擀平，放上油酥面团后包起。包好的水油皮再用保鲜膜盖起，避免流失水分。</p>
<p>Tips：收口处要用手指确实捏紧，并朝下放置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295873" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295873" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295873-0a47ec530e1f3419b28091b6ad7bdc41.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295873-0a47ec530e1f3419b28091b6ad7bdc41-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295873" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥14。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13295874" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295874" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295874-528ec802ef5b5fc47923ed4132ce531e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295874" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295874-528ec802ef5b5fc47923ed4132ce531e-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295874" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥15。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13295876" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295876" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295876-37c61ba20e08fd74e81e83484b3934d6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295876" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295876-37c61ba20e08fd74e81e83484b3934d6-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295876" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥16。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>擀压</strong></span></h3>
<p>11.将包好的水油皮面团先用手掌稍微压平后，再用擀面棍擀薄，前后端向内折三折。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295877" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295877" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295877-c77058baf11e9ffafe6d0cc1536259d0.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295877" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295877-c77058baf11e9ffafe6d0cc1536259d0-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295877" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥18。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_13295878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295878" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295878-ac6b2261dbaead87ea84754405cba7cf.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295878" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295878-ac6b2261dbaead87ea84754405cba7cf-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295878" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥19。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>12.将面团进行第二次擀平、折三折。</p>
<figure id="attachment_13295880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-13295880" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295880-b531b6a1aeba048a602e94d2648d2146.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-13295880" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2021/10/id13295880-b531b6a1aeba048a602e94d2648d2146-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-13295880" class="wp-caption-text">荷花酥20。（采实文化提供）</figcaption></figure>
