<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>含维生素C 5种打造完美肌肤的青甜椒料理</h2>

</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【小编按】不管是便当、野餐、每天的三餐，还是齐聚好友的家庭派对，通通都是色香味俱全，漂亮又美味的各类好菜，让人一看就忍不住赞叹起来。从今天起，作菜就不用伤脑筋了！从本书介绍的304种菜色中，挑选自己当下最想吃的食谱来做吧！不管是从红、黄、绿的颜色来选，还是从肉、鱼、蔬菜的菜色来考量，各式各样的搭配组合，让作<ahref="https://github.com/bpiugw352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">料理</a>的人也乐趣十足，</p>
<h1><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><ahref="https://github.com/bpiugw352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E6%A4%92.md#1">青椒</a></strong><strong>&amp;</strong><strong><ahref="https://github.com/bpiugw352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%94%9C%E6%A4%92.md#1">甜椒</a></strong></span></h1>
<p>不论哪一种都富含打造<ahref="https://github.com/bpiugw352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%8C%E7%BE%8E%E8%82%8C%E8%82%A4.md#1">完美肌肤</a>必备的<ahref="https://github.com/bpiugw352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%BB%B4%E7%94%9F%E7%B4%A0c.md#1">维生素C</a>。多彩的颜色能让配色更加活泼，为便当增添点缀。</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;"><strong>非常适合搭配中式<ahref="https://github.com/bpiugw352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%96%99%E7%90%86.md#1">料理</a>或烧卖</strong></span></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #800080;"><strong>榨菜凉拌<ahref="https://github.com/bpiugw352/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9D%92%E6%A4%92.md#1">青椒</a></strong></span></h3>
