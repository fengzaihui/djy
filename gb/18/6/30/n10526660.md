<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>1/8决赛 法国4:3胜阿根廷 姆巴佩闪亮全场</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年07月01日讯】（大纪元记者天宇报导）6月30日，<ahref="https://github.com/ppytfa315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/2018%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E6%9D%AF.md#1">2018世界杯</a>八分之一决赛第一场比赛隆重登场，<ahref="https://github.com/ppytfa315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E6%A0%B9%E5%BB%B7.md#1">阿根廷</a>对阵<ahref="https://github.com/ppytfa315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD.md#1">法国</a>，这场由上届世界杯亚军与上届欧洲杯亚军之间的强强对话，为球迷上演了进球盛宴，最终法国以4比3力克对手，晋级8强，<ahref="https://github.com/ppytfa315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%98%BF%E6%A0%B9%E5%BB%B7.md#1">阿根廷</a>铩羽而归。</p>
<p>比赛进行到仅仅10分钟，<ahref="https://github.com/ppytfa315/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD.md#1">法国</a>后场断球后发动快速反击，姆巴佩（Kylian Mbappé）从本方半场拿球后带球一直杀至对方禁区，被协防的罗霍（Marcos Rojo）绊倒，主裁当即给予黄牌加点球判罚，格里兹曼（Antoine Griezmann）一蹴而就，破门得分，法国赢得梦幻般开局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10526708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10526708" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987863536.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10526708 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987863536-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10526708" class="wp-caption-text">格里兹曼罚中点球，为法国队先拔头筹。(Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>而仅仅18分钟后，法国卷土重来，博格巴（Paul Pogba）精准长传球飞至前场，姆巴佩依靠速度优势在大禁区外率先拿球，并即将形成单刀球，塔利亚菲科（Nicolas Tagliafico）不得不将其绊倒，并因此吃到黄牌，法国得到大禁区外边沿的任意球机会，可惜博格巴的直接射门高出横梁后飞出底线。</p>
<p>双方攻防转换极快，均又有几次打门，梅西也曾被绊倒在禁区内，但裁判不予理会。直到第40分钟，迪马利亚（ángel Di María）在距离球门35码外，突然起脚冷射，球直飞右侧，门将洛里斯（Hugo Lloris）虽然及时反应，但仍无济于事，阿根廷在半场即将结束前将比分扳平为1比1。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10526713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10526713" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987921550.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10526713 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987921550-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10526713" class="wp-caption-text">迪马利亚世界波破门，帮助阿根廷半场结束前扳平比分。(Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>易地再战后开场仅仅3分钟，阿根廷在一次角球进攻中，梅西于大禁区转身冷射，球打到队友默卡多（Gabriel Mercado）身上后变线入网，阿根廷将比分反超。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10526715" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10526715" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987915772.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10526715 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987915772-450x285.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="285" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10526715" class="wp-caption-text">下半场梅西射门打到队友后变线入网，将比分一度反超。(Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>但这一优势没有保持太久，第57分钟，赫尔南德斯（Lucas Hernandez）左路下底长传，帕瓦尔（Benjamin Pavard）在大禁区外直接远射破门，这粒世界波帮助法国将比分扳平为2比2，双方又回到同一起跑线上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10526717" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10526717" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987915702.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10526717 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987915702-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10526717" class="wp-caption-text">帕瓦尔远射破门，将比分再度扳平。(Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第64分钟，法国再次将比分反超，赫尔南德斯左路横敲，马图伊迪（Blaise Matuidi）的射门虽然被防守队员挡下，姆巴佩拿球后突破至禁区左侧射门得手，3:2。</p>
<p>仅仅4分钟后，法国从球门球开始的几次短传就洞穿了阿根廷防线，吉鲁（Olivier Giroud）在大禁区外送直塞球至禁区，姆巴佩在禁区内右侧直接射门再次破门，比分改写为4比2。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10526720" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10526720" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987958252.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10526720 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987958252-450x278.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="278" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10526720" class="wp-caption-text">姆巴佩在短短5分钟内两度破门，帮助法国最终获得胜利。(Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第72分钟，法国错失一次再次扩大比分机会，在一次快攻中形成3对1的局面，结果姆巴佩在大禁区外延传球时过于草率，浪费了单刀射门机会。第84分钟阿根廷同样获得扳回一球的机会，梅西带球突入至禁区，可惜起脚瞬间身体失衡，球打飞出界。</p>
<p>伤停补时阶段第92分钟，梅西中路送出精准长传球至禁区，阿奎罗头球破门，比分改写为4比3，阿根廷赢得一线生机。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10526725" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-10526725" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987973816.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10526725 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/07/GettyImages-987973816-450x300.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="300" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-10526725" class="wp-caption-text">阿奎罗补时阶段帮助阿根廷扳回一球，可惜为时已晚。(Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>可惜所剩时间极为有限，阿根廷没能再次破门，最终比分锁定在4比3，法国力克阿根廷，成为第一个晋级8强的队伍，阿根廷惨遭淘汰出局。</p>
