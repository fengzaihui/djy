<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>大温上半年房价增速放缓</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2018年07月12日讯】（<span style="font-weight: 400;">记者余天白温哥华编译报导）</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">皇家地产公司（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Royal LePage</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）本</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">周</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">发布的最新报告指出，今年前两季度大温房价<ahref="https://github.com/cagjze345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%9E%E9%80%9F%E6%94%BE%E7%BC%93.md#1">增速放缓</a>，但房价仍明显高于去年同期水平，其主要原因是去年至今公寓价格的大幅攀</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">升</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">皇家地产公司周二发布最新房价调查报告，显示大温第二季度综合统计房价仅比今年第一季度<ahref="https://github.com/cagjze345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BB%B7%E4%B8%8A%E6%B6%A8.md#1">房价上涨</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">0.5%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，涨幅极微。但大温当前的综合房价仍比去年同期房价高出</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">7.2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，达到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1,269,816</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，其主要推手是公寓价格在过去一年中的飞涨：大温公寓综合房价目前达到</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">692,452</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，比去年同期上升</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">18.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">与去年同期相比，温哥华市<ahref="https://github.com/cagjze345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%88%BF%E4%BB%B7%E4%B8%8A%E6%B6%A8.md#1">房价上涨</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，西温房价上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">3.8%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，北温和列治文市房价分别上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">5.9%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">6%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，均低于大温房价平均涨幅。而本拿比（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">8.5%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）、高贵林（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）、素</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">16.4%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）和兰</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">市（</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">21.2%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">）房价涨幅超过大温平均水平。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">素</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">和兰</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">市当前综合房价仍低于</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">100</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万加元，素</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">市综合房价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">900,433</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，兰</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">市综合房价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">975,360</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元。素</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">里</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">市公寓综合房价为</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">381,626</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">元，同比上涨</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">25.6%</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">皇家地产公司顾问</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Adil Dinani</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">分析公寓市场称，大温房价的高昂促使大部分房产买家在购买自己的第一套住房时选择公寓，大量的需求导致公寓市场购买竞争激烈、价格上扬，而公寓价格的快速上涨又促使二套房买家和房产投资者出于投资目的购买公寓，进一步加剧了公寓价格的飞涨。</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><br />
</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“大温地区的公寓价格在继续攀</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">升</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">到前所未有的高度。”</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Dinani</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">表示，“对比而言，现在买一套公寓的钱</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年前可以在大温地区买一幢二层的独立屋了。”</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">虽然公寓市场依然相当火热，但大温房价总体增速已经下降。皇家地产公司指出，联邦政府今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月推出的“压力测试”等收紧贷款的政策和卑诗省府今年</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">二</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">2</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月推出的打击房市投机政策是房价<ahref="https://github.com/cagjze345/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%A2%9E%E9%80%9F%E6%94%BE%E7%BC%93.md#1">增速放缓</a>的主要原因。◇</span></p>
