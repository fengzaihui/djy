<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>好眼睛是养出来的 护眼茶饮DIY</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2018年06月01日讯】现在人醒来第一件事是看手机，睡前最后一件事还是看手机。因此很多人常觉得<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%BC%E7%9D%9B.md#1">眼睛</a>酸涩，视力下降很快，不知不觉间出现干眼症等眼部疾病。每天工作时拚命用、休闲时也拚命用，却忽略了保养自己的眼睛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10210574" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/iphone-410311_1280-e1520835660800.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10210574 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/03/iphone-410311_1280-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">现在人关心减重、防癌、抗老，却忽略了保养自己的<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%9C%BC%E7%9D%9B.md#1">眼睛</a>。（Pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p>若想养<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%A4%E7%9C%BC.md#1">护眼</a>睛、防治眼病，眼用药品和食疗茶饮都是良方。在常见的药材中，枸杞、菊花、决明子、桑叶都是护眼的良药。枸杞含有大量的叶黄素与粟米黄素，且有补血明目的作用；桑叶能够洗肝明目。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10439295" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/03-Fotolia_56996932_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-10439295" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/03-Fotolia_56996932_Subaion_L-450x443.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="443" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在常见的药材中，枸杞、菊花、决明子、桑叶都是<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%A4%E7%9C%BC.md#1">护眼</a>的良药。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
<p>专家建议，日常可饮用“<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%9E%B8%E6%9D%9E%E8%8F%8A%E8%8A%B1%E8%8C%B6.md#1">枸杞菊花茶</a>”，眼睛酸涩的人可以饮用“<ahref="https://github.com/oeocpl316/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A1%91%E6%9D%9E%E8%8C%B6.md#1">桑杞茶</a>”。</p>
<h2><strong>日常护眼小叮咛：</strong></h2>
<p>1. 最根本的方式还是要多让眼睛休息。2.保持眼睛湿润，避免在强光下阅读、看电脑萤幕。3.适当休息，避免熬夜。4.促进眼部血液循环，热敷放松眼部。5.糖尿病人应谨慎控制血糖。</p>
<figure id="attachment_10439296" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/02-Fotolia_110052242_Subaion_L.jpg"><img class="wp-image-10439296 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/05/02-Fotolia_110052242_Subaion_L-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">保养眼睛，最根本的方式还是要多让眼睛休息。（Fotolia）</figcaption></figure>
