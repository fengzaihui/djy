<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>加西台湾各大学校友会联欢 学子展现缤纷才华</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_0623-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>图：驻温经文处官员、加西台湾各大学校友会联合会理事、校友会会长和联络人与嘉宾们合影。（邱晨/大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【大纪元2016年12月23日讯】（大纪元记者邱晨报导）当年风华正茂的台湾30所大学的200多名佼佼学子，12月18日欢聚粤海山庄海鲜酒家，共襄第七届<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%90%84%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">台湾各大学</a><ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E8%A5%BF.md#1">加西</a><ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B%E4%BC%9A.md#1">校友会</a>联合会的圣诞餐舞会。</p>
<p>驻<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>经济文化办事处（经文处）李志强处长伉俪、侨务委员吴丽珍和邓华一、侨务主任杨修玮也风雪夜赶来，恭祝大家圣诞快乐健康，新年行大运，发大财。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8618928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8618928" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_184511_HDR-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8618928 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_184511_HDR-450x253.jpg" alt="图：驻温经文处李志强处长恭祝台湾各大学校友圣诞快乐，新年好运。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8618928" class="wp-caption-text">图：驻温经文处李志强处长恭祝<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E5%90%84%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%A6.md#1">台湾各大学</a>校友圣诞快乐，新年好运。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8618929" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8618929" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_184643_HDR-e1482432471597.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8618929" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_184643_HDR-e1482432471597.jpg" alt="图：驻温经文处侨务委员吴丽珍向台湾各大学校友祝福。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8618929" class="wp-caption-text">图：驻温经文处侨务委员吴丽珍向台湾各大学校友祝福。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>瑞雪纷飞兆丰年，餐舞会上同样是热情高涨，高潮迭起，学子们毫不吝惜展现才艺风华。侨务委员邓华一的“风雪故人来”，亦是温馨感人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8618930" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8618930" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_184746_HDR-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8618930 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_184746_HDR-450x253.jpg" alt="图：驻温经文处侨务委员邓华一以一句风雪故人来，温馨感人。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8618930" class="wp-caption-text">图：驻温经文处侨务委员邓华一以一句“风雪故人来”，温馨感人。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>作为餐舞会总召集人的<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%96%B0%E6%B1%89.md#1">徐新汉</a>，更放下豪言：“去年圣诞聚会下雨，今年下雪，明年下刀子我们照样来。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_8618954" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8618954" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_200159_HDR-e1482433289278.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8618954" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_200159_HDR-e1482433289278.jpg" alt="图：驻温侨务主任杨修玮祝福大家。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8618954" class="wp-caption-text">图：驻温侨务主任杨修玮祝福大家。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_8618952" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8618952" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_0616-e1482433153777.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8618952" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_0616-e1482433153777.jpg" alt="图：加西台湾各大学校友会总召集人徐新汉。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8618952" class="wp-caption-text">图：<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8A%A0%E8%A5%BF.md#1">加西</a>台湾各大学<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%A0%A1%E5%8F%8B%E4%BC%9A.md#1">校友会</a>总召集人<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%90%E6%96%B0%E6%B1%89.md#1">徐新汉</a>。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年参加校友会餐舞会的侨界领袖众多，有世华女企协<ahref="https://github.com/abrwif329/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%A9%E5%93%A5%E5%8D%8E.md#1">温哥华</a>分会会长游慈心伉俪、温哥华合家欢联谊会会长孙淑蕙伉俪、资深侨领和侨务咨询委员叶宪年、列治文扶轮社社长谢坚等。当晚一位特别嘉宾是台湾之光、台大校友的邱丽莲教授，她将参加明年省选，竞选列治文中心选区省议员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8618951" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8618951" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_0608-e1482432921931.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8618951" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/DSC_0608-e1482432921931.jpg" alt="图：卑诗台湾商会会长高铭发是当晚餐舞会的风云人物。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="299" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8618951" class="wp-caption-text">图：卑诗台湾商会会长高铭发是当晚餐舞会的风云人物。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>卑诗台湾商会新任会长高铭发，以一顶闪光活动的圣诞帽亮相，介绍自己创办企业的成功经验，并表达对贵人徐新汉的感恩，是现场极其活跃，幽默风趣的高富帅。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8618970" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8618970" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_202927_HDR-e1482433213951.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8618970" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_202927_HDR-e1482433213951.jpg" alt="图：台湾各大学铭大会长张丽娜正在演唱。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8618970" class="wp-caption-text">图：台湾各大学铭大会长张丽娜正在演唱。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>艺术家张丽娜不仅是周士心老师嫡传弟子，又是温哥华台湾客家会会长和铭大会长，当晚餐舞会又摇身一变成为歌唱家，还捐赠亲笔国画作为抽奖奖品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_8618933" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8618933" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_190907_HDR-e1482432563189.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="size-full wp-image-8618933" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_190907_HDR-e1482432563189.jpg" alt="图：温哥华知名小提琴家Tom，演奏小提琴曲如痴如醉。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8618933" class="wp-caption-text">图：温哥华知名小提琴家Tom，演奏小提琴曲如痴如醉。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>温哥华知名小提琴家Tom，现场演奏的小提琴曲令人如痴如醉，正好是美味佳肴的最佳佐料，而校友们一个个自动登台，欢快的唱起流行歌曲或经典歌曲，伴随着音乐旋律，大家纷纷下池翩翩起舞。</p>
<p><strong>李荣堂摘取海外著述奖</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_8618926" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-8618926" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_193329_HDR-450x253.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-8618926 size-medium" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2016/12/20161218_193329_HDR-450x253.jpg" alt="图：驻温经文处李志强处长（左一）给台南师大会长李荣堂（中）颁发海外著述佳作奖，右一为徐总召。（邱晨/大纪元）" width="450" b="253" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-8618926" class="wp-caption-text">图：驻温经文处李志强处长（左一）给台南师大会长李荣堂（中）颁发海外著述佳作奖，右一为徐总召。（邱晨/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>济济满堂的学子中，教授学者众多，台北侨联总会颁发的海外著述奖，今年由台南大学校友会会长李荣堂摘取殊荣。李荣堂称自己一生学业顺利、著作认真，曾经在美国获得一个硕士和两个博士学位，又被保送师大英语系。这次获奖，他认为归功于自己的不懈努力，一步一脚印奋斗的结果。李荣堂是一位学术创作的耕耘者，兢兢业业，学术论著长年不断。</p>
