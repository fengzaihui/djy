<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>Cupertino特色面食馆攻略</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年07月29日讯】文：勤宜?大纪元</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/kbkosb312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%9D%A2%E9%A3%9F.md#1">湾区面食</a>：<ahref="https://github.com/kbkosb312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BC%8E%E6%B3%B0%E4%B8%B0.md#1">鼎泰丰</a>Din Tai Fung</h2>
<p>在南湾这家<ahref="https://github.com/kbkosb312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BC%8E%E6%B3%B0%E4%B8%B0.md#1">鼎泰丰</a>DTF环境优雅舒适，人气也旺，高峰期排队等待是常事。有客户比较其它分店，如香港、新加坡、西雅图、南加的连锁店，感觉这家的服务最好，另有客户也非常赞赏在这里得到了VIP般的服务。这是一家集上海、台湾和四川风味的特色面食餐馆。<br />
虽然对于小笼包，客户有不同的评价，有食客觉得很正点，有食客觉得要碰运气，运气不好的话，小笼包就比较一般。辣油馄饨众食客都一致点赞，其实就是四川的红油抄手。另外有食客对他家的香芋小笼包情有独钟，每次去鼎泰丰必点。<figure id="attachment_11416144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11416144" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/11.26.57.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/11.26.57-450x527.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="527" class="size-medium wp-image-11416144" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11416144" class="wp-caption-text">鼎泰丰的小笼包、红油抄手都是必点餐。<ahref="https://github.com/kbkosb312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B9%BE%E5%8C%BA%E9%9D%A2%E9%A3%9F.md#1">湾区面食</a>，美国鼎泰丰网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11416149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11416149" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/11.26.38.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/11.26.38-450x419.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="419" class="size-medium wp-image-11416149" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11416149" class="wp-caption-text">鼎泰丰的酸辣面。（湾区面食，美国鼎泰丰网站）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>湾区面食，鼎泰丰Din Tai Fung 联络方式：<br />
地址：2855 Stevens Creek Blvd., #1259<br />
Santa Clara, CA 95050<br />
电话：408-248-1688<br />

<h2><ahref="https://github.com/kbkosb312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/cupertino%E9%9D%A2%E9%A3%9F%E9%A6%86.md#1">Cupertino面食馆</a>，大上海<br />
Shanghai Garden</h2>
<p>如果不想排队等候吃正宗的上海小笼包，对味道也还有些讲究的话，来大上海也是个不错的选择。有食客说大上海是本地区自己最喜欢的中餐馆，同时满足了家里中国人和西方人的胃口。也供应生煎包，还有一些特色小菜，糖酥排骨、柠檬鸡等上百个菜品。<figure id="attachment_11416150" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11416150" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20171221011348.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20171221011348-450x216.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="216" class="size-medium wp-image-11416150" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11416150" class="wp-caption-text">大上海的餐点。<ahref="https://github.com/kbkosb312/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/cupertino%E9%9D%A2%E9%A3%9F%E9%A6%86.md#1">Cupertino面食馆</a>，大上海网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11416151" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11416151" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20171221011341.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/20171221011341-450x216.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="216" class="size-medium wp-image-11416151" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11416151" class="wp-caption-text">大上海的菜点同时满足了中国人和西方人的胃口。（Cupertino面食馆，大上海网站）</figcaption></figure></p>
<p>Cupertino面食馆，大上海Shanghai Garden 联络方式：<br />

Cupertino, CA 95014<br />

<h2>湾区面食，上海生煎馆<br />
Shanghai Flavor Shop</h2>
<p>一个貌似家常的上海风味小吃店，生煎包是食客们的最爱，老顾客们从不厌倦，觉得他们美味多汁热呼呼的生煎包很疗愈，让人胃口大开，感觉很上瘾。在南湾也是颇有名气，不是最好也是最好之一吧！也有个别客户抱怨他们服务和食品，大部分客户还是蛮认可的，即便是工作日的时候，小小的店堂也时常座无虚席。<figure id="attachment_11416154" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11416154" style="width: 278px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/S_for-proofread_0720_D12_Leo_Regina1.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/S_for-proofread_0720_D12_Leo_Regina1.jpg" alt="" width="278" b="162" class="size-full wp-image-11416154" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11416154" class="wp-caption-text">生煎包。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11416156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11416156" style="width: 281px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/S_for-proofread_0720_D12.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/S_for-proofread_0720_D12.jpg" alt="" width="281" b="189" class="size-full wp-image-11416156" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11416156" class="wp-caption-text">卤肉面，类似上海生煎馆的焖肉面。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure></p>

<h2>Cupertino面食馆，梁妈妈眷村小吃<br />
Liang’s Village Cupertino</h2>
<p>这是一家荟萃了东西南北面食的特色餐馆，牛肉、牛筋干拌面、咸水鸭面、炸酱面、凉面、排骨面、肉末米粉等等，喜欢面条的去这家应该是很满足的。她家的各种奶茶、水果饮料也很丰富，一位曾经为自己女儿订过公园生日宴的家长，对她家的食品，特别是饮料赞赏有加。<figure id="attachment_11416158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11416158" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/12.48.23.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/12.48.23-450x478.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="478" class="size-medium wp-image-11416158" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11416158" class="wp-caption-text">梁妈妈家的各种面食。（Cupertino面食馆，梁妈妈眷村小吃网站）</figcaption></figure><figure id="attachment_11416159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11416159" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/12.48.52.jpg"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/07/12.48.52-450x415.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="415" class="size-medium wp-image-11416159" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11416159" class="wp-caption-text">梁妈妈家的饮品也令食客称道。（Cupertino面食馆，梁妈妈眷村小吃网站）</figcaption></figure></p>
