<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>六夺超级杯冠军 爱国者波士顿胜利大游行</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-1-1NFL-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>估计超过百万球迷涌进波士顿，热烈欢迎第六次捧回美式足球“超级杯”冠军的新英格兰爱国者队（New England Patriots）凯旋归来。（黄剑宇／大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年02月06日讯】（大纪元<span class="s1">记者刘景烨波士顿报导）</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">5</span><span class="s1">日，迎着15℃</span><span class="s1">的暖风，新英格兰<ahref="https://github.com/lseeig3883/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%9B%BD%E8%80%85.md#1">爱国者</a>队（</span><span class="s2">New England Patriots）</span><span class="s1">乘“鸭子船”向波士顿市政府大楼进发，与上百万粉丝分享他们第六次捧回“<ahref="https://github.com/lseeig3883/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E7%BA%A7%E6%9D%AF.md#1">超级杯</a>”的喜悦。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">月</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">日晚的第</span><span class="s2">53</span><span class="s1">届美式足球“<ahref="https://github.com/lseeig3883/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%B6%85%E7%BA%A7%E6%9D%AF.md#1">超级杯</a>”大赛中，<ahref="https://github.com/lseeig3883/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%88%B1%E5%9B%BD%E8%80%85.md#1">爱国者</a>队以</span><span class="s2">13</span><span class="s1">比</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">的成绩战胜了<ahref="https://github.com/lseeig3883/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6%E5%85%AC%E7%BE%8A%E9%98%9F.md#1">洛杉矶公羊队</a>，缔造了平历史纪录的佳绩，之前的纪录是匹兹堡钢人队创造的。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">欢呼的粉丝挤满了</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">哩的<ahref="https://github.com/lseeig3883/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>路线，也令周边的火车、地铁人满为患。麻州捷运署（</span><span class="s2">MBTA</span><span class="s1">）表示，当天的乘客显着多于过去的每次<ahref="https://github.com/lseeig3883/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B8%B8%E8%A1%8C.md#1">游行</a>，“几乎是我们在公交网络中见过的最大的乘客数量”。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11026845" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11026845" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref=" https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-1-5NFL-600x338.jpg" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"> <img class="wp-image-11026845 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-1-5NFL-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11026845" class="wp-caption-text">上百万爱国者队粉丝夹道欢迎该队捧回第53届美式足球“超级杯”冠军杯。（刘景烨／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">尽管没有人正式统计参加人数，波士顿警局估计这一数字可能接近百万，也有估计</span><span class="s2">150</span><span class="s1">万。波士顿公共学校表示，当天的缺课学生竟比平常多了</span><span class="s2">2000人</span><span class="s1">。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">尽管游行上午</span><span class="s2">11</span><span class="s1">点才开始，粉丝们</span><span class="s2">9</span><span class="s1">点就开始聚集在游行线路上。他们吹响号角，高呼“</span><span class="s2">Go Pats</span><span class="s1">”、“我们再次战胜洛杉矶（</span><span class="s2">We beat LA again</span><span class="s1">）”，迎接爱国者队到来。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">当明星四分卫<ahref="https://github.com/lseeig3883/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E9%9B%B7%E8%BF%AA.md#1">布雷迪</a>（</span><span class="s2">Tom Brady</span><span class="s1">）经过时，人们激动地大喊他的名字，还高呼“我们要第七次（</span><span class="s2">We want seven!</span><span class="s1">）”，期盼爱国者队再续辉煌。带着儿女游行的<ahref="https://github.com/lseeig3883/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%B8%83%E9%9B%B7%E8%BF%AA.md#1">布雷迪</a>穿上印有“</span><span class="s2">6</span><span class="s1">”字的衣服，庆贺他与爱国者队的每一次“超级杯”冠军。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11026837" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11026837" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-1-2NFL.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11026837" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-1-2NFL-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11026837" class="wp-caption-text">创造纪录的明星四分卫布雷迪（Tom Brady）高举奖杯向群众致意。（黄剑宇/大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">布雷迪今年已经</span><span class="s2">41</span><span class="s1">岁了。除了</span><span class="s2">6</span><span class="s1">次冠军之外，他还曾</span><span class="s2">4</span><span class="s1">度斩获</span><span class="s2">MVP</span><span class="s1">殊<span class="s2">（</span>最有价值球员）荣。他曾对媒体表示，他的目标是要在球场上奋战到</span><span class="s2">45</span><span class="s1">岁。</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">另一位明星则是爱国者队的接球员艾德曼（</span><span class="s2">Julian Edelman）</span><span class="s1">在比赛中</span><span class="s2">10</span><span class="s1">次接球、推进</span><span class="s2">141</span><span class="s1">码，出色的表现让他获得本届超级杯</span><span class="s2">MVP</span><span class="s1">。艾德曼也是</span><span class="s2">NFL</span><span class="s1">史上第</span><span class="s2">7</span><span class="s1">位接球员获得超级杯“最有价值球员”的殊荣，他也是爱国者队史上第</span><span class="s2">2</span><span class="s1">位获得超级杯</span><span class="s2">MVP</span><span class="s1">的接球员。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11026838" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11026838" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-1-3NFL.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11026838 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-1-3NFL-e1549416083655-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11026838" class="wp-caption-text">爱国者队的接球员艾德曼（Julian Edelman，着红衣拿奖杯者）获得本届超级杯MVP（最有价值球员）。（黄剑宇／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">此外，爱国者队亦有首位进入“超级杯”的华裔球员钟家庭（</span><span class="s2">Patrick Chung</span><span class="s1">）。他出生在牙买加，祖父是中国移民。从</span><span class="s2">2009</span><span class="s1">年到</span><span class="s2">2018</span><span class="s1">年，他先后为新英格兰爱国者队和费城老鹰队效力，主要担任防守球员，并曾随爱国者队</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">次夺得“超级杯”。</span></p>
<figure id="attachment_11026865" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-11026865" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a target="_blank" href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-1-4NFL-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11026865 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/02/1-1-4NFL-1-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-11026865" class="wp-caption-text">爱国者队亦有首位进入“超级杯”的华裔球员钟家庭（Patrick Chung，球衣31号）。（黄剑宇／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">在</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">日晚的比赛中，钟家庭于第</span><span class="s2">3</span><span class="s1">节因手臂骨折而下场。绑上石膏后，他又回到赛场为队友加油。“我必须支持我的队友，在更衣室做不了这个。”他对</span><span class="s2">NESN</span><span class="s1">记者说。爱国者主教练</span><span class="s2">Bill Belichick</span><span class="s1">在赛后新闻会中说：“每个人都喜欢钟家庭。他是我教过的最棒的球员之一。”◇#</span></p>
